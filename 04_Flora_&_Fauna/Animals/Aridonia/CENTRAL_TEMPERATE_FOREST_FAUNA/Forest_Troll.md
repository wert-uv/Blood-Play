# Forest Troll (*Trollus silvanus*)

- **Type:** Animal
- **Biome:** CENTRAL_TEMPERATE_FOREST_FAUNA

- **Size:** 2.5-3 meters tall, 400-600 kg
- **Distribution:** Remote old-growth forests
- **Characteristics:** Moss-covered hide, regeneration ability
- **Behavior:** Solitary, territorial, hibernates in winter
- **Diet:** Omnivorous - prefers meat but eats anything
- **Danger Level:** Extreme - regeneration makes them very hard to kill
- **Intelligence:** Low but cunning; can use simple tools
- **Weaknesses:** Fire prevents regeneration; acid damage
- **Cultural Note:** Feature in cautionary tales; bounties offered
