# Forest Boar (*Sus ferox*)

- **Type:** Animal
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Size:** 1.2 meters length, 150-250 kg
- **Distribution:** All forest biomes except Sprout Kingdom

- **Behavior:** Sounders (groups) of females; males solitary except breeding
