# Mistwalker Bear (*Ursus nebulosus*)

- **Type:** Animal
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Size:** 2-3 meters standing, 300-500 kg
- **Distribution:** Dense forests of Sprout Kingdom, northern Cerulea, Kingdom of Rust
- **Characteristics:** Silver-gray fur blends with fog, nearly silent movement
- **Magical Properties:** Can become partially ethereal in thick mist
