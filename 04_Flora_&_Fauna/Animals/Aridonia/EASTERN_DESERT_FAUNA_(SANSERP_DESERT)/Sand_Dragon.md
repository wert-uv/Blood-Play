# Sand Dragon (*Draco arenae*)

- **Type:** Animal
- **Biome:** EASTERN_DESERT_FAUNA_(SASSERT_DESERT)

- **Size:** 5-7 meters length, 800-1200 kg
- **Distribution:** Deep desert of Vorth Empire
- **Characteristics:** Tan-gold scales, can burrow through sand, breathes superheated sand
- **Magical Properties:** Strongly magical; can sense vibrations through sand
- **Behavior:** Solitary except mating, ambush hunters, territorial (100+ km²)
- **Diet:** Large mammals, camels, occasionally humanoids
- **Danger Level:** Extreme - apex predator of the desert
- **Cultural Significance:** Sacred to Vorth Empire; dragon-riders bond with them
- **Status:** Rare; protected by empire (perhaps 200 individuals)
