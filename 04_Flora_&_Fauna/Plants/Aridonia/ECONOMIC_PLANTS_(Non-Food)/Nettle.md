# Nettle

*Urtica dioica*
Biome: ECONOMIC PLANTS (Non-Food)
Sub-Biome: FIBER CROPS

- Fiber: Very fine, linen-like when processed
- Challenge: Stinging hairs (processing removes them)
- Uses: Fine cloth, cordage
- Nutrition: Young leaves edible, nutritious
