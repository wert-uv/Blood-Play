# Westallis Hazel (*Corylus occidentalis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Forest edges, clearings in Westallis
- **Height:** 3-6 meters
- **Nuts:** Round, enclosed in leafy husk
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Spreads vegetatively, good wildlife food

### Additional Info
- Hazelnuts: food, oil
- Dowsing rods (folk tradition)
- Coppiced for basket materials
