# White Ash (*Fraxinus candida*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Height: 25-30 meters, straight trunk
- Wood: Strong, flexible, shock-resistant
- Uses: Weapon handles (spears, axes), tool handles, furniture
- Ecology: Deep roots, improves soil
- Threatened: By ash blight disease spreading from east
