# Serviceberry (*Amelanchier communis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Early spring, white, delicate
- Berries: Purple-blue, sweet, edible (June)
- Cultural: Berry ripening marks "service season" (travel, gathering)
