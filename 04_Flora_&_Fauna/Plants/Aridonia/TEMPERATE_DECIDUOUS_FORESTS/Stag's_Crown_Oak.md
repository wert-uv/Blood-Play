# Stag's Crown Oak (*Quercus cervidae*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Dominant across Kingdom of Debrica (gives kingdom its name)
- **Characteristics:** Antler-like branch patterns, golden-brown autumn leaves
- **Height:** 25-35 meters
- **Lifespan:** 300-500 years
- **Cultural Use:** Sacred to Debrican culture, massive specimens mark clan territories
- **Wildlife:** Acorns are primary food for deer populations
- **Wood Properties:** Hard, durable, excellent for furniture and construction
