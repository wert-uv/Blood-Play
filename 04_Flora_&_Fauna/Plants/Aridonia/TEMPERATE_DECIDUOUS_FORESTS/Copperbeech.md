# Copperbeech (*Fagus cupreum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Mixed throughout central forests, prefers limestone soils
- **Characteristics:** Leaves turn brilliant copper in autumn, bark has coppery tinge
- **Magical Properties:** Leaves can temporarily store copper-based spell components
- **Height:** 20-30 meters
- **Lifespan:** 200-400 years
- **Cultural Use:** Leaves dried and used as spell paper by Debrica hedge mages
- **Ecology:** Beechnuts critical for wildlife; mast years cause population booms
