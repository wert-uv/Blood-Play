# Shadowberry (*Vaccinium obscurum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Common in deep forest understory
- **Characteristics:** Dark purple berries, prefers shaded understory
- **Magical Properties:** Berries grant darkvision for 1 hour when eaten
- **Height:** 0.5-1.5 meters
- **Alchemy:** Primary ingredient in shadow magic potions
- **Culinary:** Too bitter to eat raw; made into preserves
