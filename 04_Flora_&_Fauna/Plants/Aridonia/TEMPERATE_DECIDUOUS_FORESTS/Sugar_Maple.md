# Sugar Maple (*Acer saccharum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Sap: Harvested in early spring, boiled to syrup
- Fall Color: Brilliant orange, red, yellow
- Uses: Syrup (major trade commodity), hardwood for floors
- Ecology: Produces heavy shade, inhibits understory
- Cultural: Tapping season is major festival time
