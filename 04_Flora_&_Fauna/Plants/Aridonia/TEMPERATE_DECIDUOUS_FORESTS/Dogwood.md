# Dogwood (*Cornus floridus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Large white/pink bracts in spring (before leaves)
- Berries: Red, eaten by birds
- Wood: Extremely hard, used for small tools
- Cultural: Flowering marks planting season
