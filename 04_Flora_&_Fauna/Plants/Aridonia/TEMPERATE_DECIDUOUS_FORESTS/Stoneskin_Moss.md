# Stoneskin Moss (*Bryophyta petra*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Rock outcrops, old ruins, stone walls
- **Characteristics:** Gray moss that hardens to stone-like texture when dry
- **Magical Properties:** Grants +1 AC for 8 hours when consumed
- **Cultural Use:** Soldiers chew it before battle
- **Ecology:** Breaks down rock into soil over decades
