# Elderberry (*Sambucus medicinalis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Flowers: White, umbrella-shaped clusters, fragrant
- Berries: Purple-black, must be cooked (raw berries toxic)
- Uses: Wine, syrup, medicine (immune support, fever reducer)
- Folklore: Protective spirit dwells in elder trees
