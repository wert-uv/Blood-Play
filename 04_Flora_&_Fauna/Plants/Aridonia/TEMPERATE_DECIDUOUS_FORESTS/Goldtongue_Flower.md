# Goldtongue Flower (*Ranunculus linguae*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Scattered throughout zone, never abundant
- **Characteristics:** Bright yellow petals, grows in damp clearings
- **Magical Properties:** Flowers grant ability to speak any language for 10 minutes
- **Height:** 10-30 cm
- **Cultural Value:** Extremely valuable; over-harvesting nearly caused extinction
- **Cultivation:** Now protected; licensed cultivation only
