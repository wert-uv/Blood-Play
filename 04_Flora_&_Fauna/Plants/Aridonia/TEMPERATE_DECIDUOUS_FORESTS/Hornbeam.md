- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Wood: Hardest of all deciduous trees
- Uses: Gears, mallets, butcher blocks
- Form: Often grows as hedge or understory
