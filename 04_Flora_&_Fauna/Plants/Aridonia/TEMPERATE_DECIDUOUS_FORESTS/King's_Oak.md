# King's Oak (*Quercus regalis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Size: Massive, up to 35m tall, 3m diameter
- Longevity: Lives 400-600 years
- Bark: Deeply furrowed, gray-brown
- Acorns: Large, important food source
- Ecology: Supports 300+ species of insects, birds, mammals
- Cultural: Sacred to royalty, coronation sites, boundary markers
- Uses: Highest quality timber, barrels for aging wine/spirits
