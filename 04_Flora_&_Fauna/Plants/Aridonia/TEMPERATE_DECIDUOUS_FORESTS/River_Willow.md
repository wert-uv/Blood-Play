# River Willow (*Salix fluvius*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Every river and stream bank in the zone
- **Characteristics:** Drooping branches, grows along all waterways
- **Magical Properties:** Bark tea suppresses minor curses and fevers
- **Height:** 15-25 meters
- **Lifespan:** 80-150 years
- **Cultural Use:** Branches used for basket weaving, wands, divining water
- **Ecology:** Roots stabilize riverbanks; critical erosion control
