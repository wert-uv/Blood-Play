# Black Walnut (*Juglans obscurus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Nuts: Valuable food source, difficult to crack
- Wood: Dark, rich brown, highly prized
- Allelopathy: Produces juglone, inhibits nearby plant growth
- Uses: Fine furniture, gunstocks, edible nuts, black dye from hulls
