# Ironwood Hazel (*Corylus ferrum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Throughout central forests, particularly in Debrica
- **Characteristics:** Dense wood that dulls steel axes, produces nutritious nuts
- **Height:** 4-8 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Nuts are staple food when milled into flour
- **Wood Properties:** Too hard for most uses but excellent for tool handles
