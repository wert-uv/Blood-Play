# Highland Spruce (*Picea montana*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Northern Westallis, mixed with beech
- **Height:** 25-35 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 0.8-1.5 meters
- **Needles:** Short, sharp, blue-green
- **Cones:** Cylindrical, 8-12cm, pendulous
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Intolerant of shade, requires disturbance

### Additional Info
- Construction timber
- Musical instruments (especially soundboards)
- Christmas tree equivalent in winter celebrations
