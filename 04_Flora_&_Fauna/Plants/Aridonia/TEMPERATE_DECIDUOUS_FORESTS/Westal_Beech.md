# Westal Beech (*Fagus westallis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Kingdom of Westallis lowlands
- **Height:** 30-40 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 1-2 meters
- **Bark:** Smooth, light gray (elephant-skin texture)
- **Leaves:** Ovate, toothed margins, golden yellow in autumn
- **Nuts:** Triangular beechnuts, edible
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Shade-tolerant, dominates mid-succession

### Additional Info
- Furniture wood (beautiful pale grain)
- Nuts: oil extraction, animal feed
- Bark: parchment substitute in emergencies
