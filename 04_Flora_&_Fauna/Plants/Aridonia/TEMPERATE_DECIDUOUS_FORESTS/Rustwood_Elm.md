# Rustwood Elm (*Ulmus oxidium*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Kingdom of Rust (gave region its name), northern Westallis
- **Characteristics:** Reddish-brown bark, wood develops rust-colored streaks with age
- **Height:** 25-40 meters
- **Lifespan:** 150-300 years
- **Cultural Use:** Traditional building material; association with honest labor
- **Disease Note:** Suffering from Rust Blight; many ancient trees dying
- **Wood Properties:** Tough, resistant to splitting, used in industrial applications
