# Wild Rose (*Rosa sylvestris*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Pink, five-petaled, fragrant
- Hips: Orange-red, high in vitamin C, persist through winter
- Uses: Tea, jams, medicine, perfume
- Thorns: Effective defense
