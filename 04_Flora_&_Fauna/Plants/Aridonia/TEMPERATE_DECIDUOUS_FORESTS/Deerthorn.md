# Deerthorn (*Crataegus cervina*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Forest edges in Debrica, forms natural fencing
- **Characteristics:** Long thorns curve like antler points, white spring flowers
- **Height:** 3-6 meters
- **Wildlife:** Deer browse leaves despite thorns; birds nest in protective branches
- **Cultural Use:** Living fences planted by farmers
