# Hawthorn (*Crataegus spinosa*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** TEMPERATE_DECIDUOUS_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Thorns: Long, sharp, defensive
- Flowers: White, fragrant (May)
- Berries: Red, edible, medicinal (heart tonic)
- Folklore: Fairy trees, never cut alone specimen
- Uses: Hedgerows, medicine, wine
