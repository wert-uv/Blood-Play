# Copper Beech

*Fagus cupreus*
Biome: TEMPERATE DECIDUOUS FORESTS
Sub-Biome: MIXED HARDWOOD ZONE (Kingdom of Rust, FylloDraya, Kingdom of Debrica, Northern Westallis)

- Leaves: Copper-red in spring and fall, green in summer
- Bark: Smooth, gray, often carved with lovers' initials
- Size: 25-30 meters
- Ecology: Produces mast crop every 3-5 years (beechnuts)
- Uses: Furniture, tool handles, edible nuts (roasted)
