# Rock-Moss Cushion (*Saxifraga bryoides*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Rock crevices above 2500m
- **Height:** 2-5cm, forms dense cushions
- **Flowers:** Tiny, yellow, cover entire plant
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Extremely slow-growing (1mm per year)

### Additional Info
- Indicates permanent water sources
