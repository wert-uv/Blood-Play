# Dwarf Mountain Pine (*Pinus montana minor*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Treeline ecotone 2,200-2,500m
- **Characteristics:** Grows horizontally due to wind, forms dense mats
- **Height:** 1-3 meters (krummholz form)
- **Ecology:** Provides windbreak for smaller alpine plants
- **Cultural Use:** Resin collected by dwarven alchemists
