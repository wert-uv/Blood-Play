# Stonecrop (*Sedum petrosum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Form: Succulent, mat-forming
- Flowers: Yellow, star-shaped
- Ecology: Grows directly on rock faces
- Adaptation: Stores water, tolerates desiccation
- Uses: Edible (salad green), medicinal (wound healing)
