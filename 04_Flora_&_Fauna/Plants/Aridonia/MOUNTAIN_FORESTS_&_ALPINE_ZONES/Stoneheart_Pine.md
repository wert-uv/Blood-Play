# Stoneheart Pine (*Pinus petros*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Dominant 1,000-1,800m elevation throughout central mountains
- **Characteristics:** Extremely dense wood, grows at harsh angles on mountain slopes
- **Magical Properties:** Wood naturally resistant to fire (physical and magical)
- **Height:** 30-45 meters
- **Lifespan:** 500-900 years
- **Cultural Use:** Dwarven construction timber, mine shaft support beams
- **Wood Properties:** Nearly as hard as softwood gets, fire-resistant
