# Silver Fir (*Abies argentea*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 25-35 meters, perfectly conical
- Needles: Silver-white undersides (snow camouflage)
- Bark: Smooth, silver-gray, blisters of aromatic resin
- Uses: Construction timber, resin for waterproofing
- Ecology: Tolerates heavy shade when young
