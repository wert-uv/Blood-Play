# Moss Campion (*Silene acaulis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Form: Dense cushion, can be centuries old
- Flowers: Pink, cover cushion in summer
- Ecology: Provides microhabitat for insects
- Adaptation: Cushion form conserves heat
