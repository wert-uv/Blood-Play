# Desert Needlegrass (*Stipa aridus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Interspersed with sagebrush throughout zone
- **Characteristics:** Sharp seed awns, forms tussocks
- **Height:** 0.3-0.8 meters
- **Ecology:** Seeds critical food for rodents and birds
- **Fire Ecology:** Highly flammable; evolved with periodic fire
