# Ironbark Spruce (*Picea ferrata*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Dwarven territories, particularly around Ayarson
- **Characteristics:** Dark gray bark contains high iron content, wood is reddish
- **Magical Properties:** Wood can hold runes longer than any other material
- **Height:** 25-40 meters
- **Lifespan:** 400-700 years
- **Cultural Use:** Sacred to dwarves; bark shavings used in iron smelting rituals
- **Wood Properties:** Dense, straight-grained, perfect for carving
