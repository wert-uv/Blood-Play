# Loge Edelweiss (*Leontopodium logensis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Vorth Loge highlands, Ayarson peaks
- **Height:** 10-20cm
- **Flowers:** Star-shaped, white woolly bracts
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Extreme cold tolerance, requires well-drained soil

### Additional Info
- Vorth Loge heraldic symbol
- Harvesting requires climbing skill (prestige gift)
- Medicinal: altitude sickness treatment
