# Mountain Ash (*Sorbus montanus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 10-15 meters, often multi-trunked
- Berries: Bright orange-red, extremely bitter but edible when cooked
- Fall Color: Brilliant red-orange
- Uses: Berries for preserves, wood for tool handles
- Ecology: Important winter food for birds
