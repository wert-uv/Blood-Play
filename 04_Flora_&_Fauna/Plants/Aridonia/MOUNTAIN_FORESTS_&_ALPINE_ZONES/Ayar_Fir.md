# Ayar Fir (*Abies ayarensis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Subalpine belt, all major mountains
- **Height:** 25-35 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 1-2 meters
- **Needles:** Short, dark green, white stripes underneath
- **Cones:** Upright, 10-15cm, purple when young
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Snow-load tolerant, shallow roots

### Additional Info
- Dwarven construction timber (straight grain, rot-resistant)
- Boughs used in winter festivals
- Needle tea: high in vitamin C
