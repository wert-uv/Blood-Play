# Bloodspine Cactus (*Opuntia sanguineus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Rocky slopes in transition zone
- **Characteristics:** Red spines, stores water, produces edible fruit
- **Magical Properties:** Spines absorb blood magic; turned crimson by exposure
- **Height:** 1-3 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Vorthic blood mages cultivate it; fruit is delicacy
- **Danger:** Spines poisonous - cause hallucinations
