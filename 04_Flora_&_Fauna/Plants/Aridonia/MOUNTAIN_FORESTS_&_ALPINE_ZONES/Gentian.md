# Gentian (*Gentiana alpina*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Deep blue, trumpet-shaped
- Adaptation: Closes in cold/rain, opens in sun
- Uses: Medicinal (digestive bitter), ornamental
- Cultural: Associated with truth and loyalty
