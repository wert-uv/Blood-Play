# Mountainblood Juniper (*Juniperus sanguineus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** 1,800-2,400m, often last woody plant before true alpine
- **Characteristics:** Reddish berries, twisted trunk form at treeline
- **Height:** 2-5 meters (often prostrate at high elevation)
- **Cultural Use:** Berries flavor mountain spirits (alcohol)
- **Wildlife:** Critical food for mountain goats and birds
