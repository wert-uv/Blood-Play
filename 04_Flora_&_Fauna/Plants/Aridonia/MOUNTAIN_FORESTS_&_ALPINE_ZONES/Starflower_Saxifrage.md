# Starflower Saxifrage (*Saxifraga astrum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Highest peaks of central mountains
- **Characteristics:** White star-shaped flowers, grows in rock crevices
- **Magical Properties:** Flowers bloom only during celestial events; petals used in divination
- **Height:** 5-10 cm
- **Cultural Value:** Extremely rare; worth 50gp per flower
- **Collection:** Only accessible to experienced mountaineers
