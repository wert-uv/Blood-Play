# Stonepine (*Pinus petrosum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 20-30 meters at lower elevations, stunted at treeline
- Adaptation: Flexible trunk bends under snow load
- Cones: Large, wingless seeds (dispersed by birds)
- Ecology: Pioneer species after avalanches
- Uses: Edible seeds (pine nuts), resinous wood for torches
- Lifespan: 300-500 years
