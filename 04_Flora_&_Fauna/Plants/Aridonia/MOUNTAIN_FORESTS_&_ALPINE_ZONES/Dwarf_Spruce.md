# Dwarf Spruce (*Picea nana*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 5-10 meters (dwarf form near treeline)
- Adaptation: Dense, compact crown resists snow damage
- Ecology: Forms krummholz (twisted wood) at treeline
- Uses: Small construction, firewood (burns hot)
