# Mountain Pine (*Pinus montanus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Treeline zones (2000-2500m)
- **Height:** 15-25 meters (stunted at treeline)
- **Form:** Twisted, gnarled at high elevations
- **Needles:** Short, stiff, bundles of 2
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Extremely cold-hardy, defines treeline

### Additional Info
- Krummholz wood prized for carving (interesting grain)
- Pioneer species for avalanche restoration
