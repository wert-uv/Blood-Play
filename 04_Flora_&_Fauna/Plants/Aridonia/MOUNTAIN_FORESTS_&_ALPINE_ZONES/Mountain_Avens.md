# Mountain Avens (*Dryas octopetala*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Alpine zones, disturbed areas above treeline
- **Height:** 5-10cm, mat-forming
- **Flowers:** White, 8-petaled, track the sun
- **Leaves:** Evergreen, small, leathery
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Nitrogen-fixing, stabilizes loose rock

### Additional Info
- Dwarven tea plant
- Pioneer species for disturbed mining sites
