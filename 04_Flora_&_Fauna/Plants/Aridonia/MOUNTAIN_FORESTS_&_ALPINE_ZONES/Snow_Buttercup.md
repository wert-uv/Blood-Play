# Snow Buttercup (*Ranunculus glacialis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Snowbed habitats, all high mountains
- **Height:** 5-15cm
- **Flowers:** White to pink, bloom near melting snow
- **Blooming:** One of the first spring flowers (May-July at altitude)
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Can photosynthesize at -7°C

### Additional Info
- Signals safe travel season in high passes
- Mildly toxic (livestock avoid it)
