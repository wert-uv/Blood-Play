# Sagebrush (*Artemisia tridentata*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Dominant across entire rain shadow zone
- **Characteristics:** Silver-gray leaves, aromatic, drought-resistant
- **Height:** 0.5-2 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Vorthic cultures burn it in cleansing ceremonies
- **Wildlife:** Primary browse for desert antelope
- **Coverage:** Can be 40-60% of ground cover
