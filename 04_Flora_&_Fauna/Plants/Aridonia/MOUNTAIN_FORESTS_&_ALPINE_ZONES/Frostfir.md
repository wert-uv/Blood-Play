# Frostfir (*Abies glacialis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** 1,500-2,200m, highest elevation tree species
- **Characteristics:** Silver-blue needles, bark produces natural anti-freeze sap
- **Magical Properties:** Needles provide resistance to cold damage
- **Height:** 20-35 meters
- **Lifespan:** 300-600 years
- **Cultural Use:** Needles brewed into tea by mountain dwellers
- **Ecology:** Provides critical winter cover for mountain wildlife
