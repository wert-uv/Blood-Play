# Dragon's Breath Lichen (*Cladonia draconis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Windswept peaks above 2,800m
- **Characteristics:** Red-orange crust lichen on exposed rock
- **Magical Properties:** When burned, produces smoke that reveals invisible creatures
- **Cultural Use:** Vorthic dragon-hunters harvest it ceremonially
- **Ecology:** Primary rock colonizer; centuries old specimens common
