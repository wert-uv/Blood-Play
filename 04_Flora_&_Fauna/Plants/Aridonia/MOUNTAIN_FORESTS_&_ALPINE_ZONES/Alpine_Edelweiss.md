# Alpine Edelweiss (*Leontopodium alpinum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Appearance: Woolly white "petals" (actually bracts)
- Ecology: Grows on dangerous cliffs
- Cultural: Symbol of courage, given to prove devotion
- Uses: Medicinal tea, decorative
- Rarity: Overharvested in accessible areas
