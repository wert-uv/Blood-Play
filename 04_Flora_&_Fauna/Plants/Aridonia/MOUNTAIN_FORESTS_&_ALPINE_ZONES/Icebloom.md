# Icebloom (*Glacialis florens*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MOUNTAIN_FORESTS_&_ALPINE_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Pure white with ice-blue center
- Blooms: Immediately after snowmelt (can bloom through thin snow)
- Adaptation: Antifreeze proteins in cells
- Uses: Rare, harvested for potions of cold resistance
- Protected: Sacred to mountain dwellers
