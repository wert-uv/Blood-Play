# Sand Sage (*Salvia arenaria*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Sandy areas throughout desert
- **Characteristics:** Deep taproots (up to 10m), small leaves, blue flowers
- **Magical Properties:** Leaves when burned produce smoke that purifies water
- **Height:** 0.3-1 meter
- **Cultural Use:** Essential for desert survival; Vorthic people never travel without it
- **Alchemy:** Used in water-finding spells
