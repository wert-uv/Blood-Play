# Saltbush (*Atriplex confertifolia*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Adaptation: Excretes salt through leaves (white coating)
- Ecology: Tolerates saline soils
- Uses: Browse for desert livestock (must limit salt intake)
- Indicator: Presence indicates saline soil conditions
