# Desert Gold (*Geraea canescens*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Bright yellow daisies, carpet desert floor
- Lifecycle: Germinates, grows, flowers, sets seed in 6-8 weeks
- Seeds: Remain dormant in soil for years (some decades)
