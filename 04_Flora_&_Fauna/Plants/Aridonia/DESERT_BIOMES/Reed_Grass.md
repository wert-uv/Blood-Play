# Reed Grass (*Phragmites communis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Every oasis and water source
- **Characteristics:** Dense stands in water margins
- **Height:** 2-4 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Building material, boat-making, baskets
- **Ecology:** Water filtration, habitat for insects and birds
