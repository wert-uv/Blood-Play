# Dragon Scale Lichen (*Collema draconis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Characteristics:** Black crust covering sand and rocks
- **Ecology:** Forms critical biological soil crust; takes 50+ years to develop
- **Importance:** Stabilizes sand, fixes nitrogen, allows other plants to establish
- **Danger:** Extremely fragile; one footstep destroys decades of growth
- **Cultural Note:** Vorthic people have strict laws protecting it
