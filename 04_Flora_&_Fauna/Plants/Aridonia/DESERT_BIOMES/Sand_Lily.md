# Sand Lily (*Hesperocallis aridus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Sandy areas; dormant for years between rains
- **Characteristics:** Bulb plant; flowers only after rain
- **Magical Properties:** Bulbs can store water magically (hold 10L in fist-sized bulb)
- **Height:** 0.3-0.6 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Emergency water source for desert travelers
- **Harvest:** Taking bulb kills plant; strictly regulated by Vorth Empire
