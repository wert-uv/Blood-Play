# Scorpion Weed (*Phacelia scorpioides*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Desert washes and temporary watercourses
- **Characteristics:** Coiled flower heads like scorpion tail, sticky leaves trap insects
- **Danger Level:** Low, but irritating to touch
- **Height:** 0.2-0.5 meters
- **Ecology:** Carnivorous adaptation; supplements nutrients from poor soil
