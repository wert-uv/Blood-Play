# Ghost Grass (*Aristida spectralis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Areas with deep water table
- **Height:** 20-40cm
- **Form:** Pale, almost white stems and leaves
- **Seeds:** Three-awned, catch wind
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Deep roots tap relict moisture

### Additional Info
- Indicator of ancient water channels
- Seeds eaten by desert people (labor-intensive)
- Dried for decoration (ghostly appearance)
