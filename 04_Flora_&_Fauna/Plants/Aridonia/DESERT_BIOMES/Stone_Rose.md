# Stone Rose (*Dudleya saxatilis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Size:** 0.1-0.3 meters diameter
- **Distribution:** Rocky desert areas, particularly near Vorth Loge
- **Characteristics:** Succulent rosette, grows on cliff faces and rock outcrops
- **Cultural Use:** Ornamental; wealthy Vorthic nobles cultivate them
- **Ecology:** Can absorb moisture from fog and dew
