# Dragon's Blood Acacia (*Acacia draconis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Scattered individual trees, concentrated near oases
- **Characteristics:** Umbrella-shaped crown, deep roots (up to 30m), red sap
- **Magical Properties:** Sap is key ingredient in dragon-summoning rituals
- **Height:** 3-8 meters (where groundwater accessible)
- **Cultural Use:** Sacred to Vorthic dragon-riders; cutting one is capital crime
- **Ecology:** Provides critical shade and nesting sites for desert birds
