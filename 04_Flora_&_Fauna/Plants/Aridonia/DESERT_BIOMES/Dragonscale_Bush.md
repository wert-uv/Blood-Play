# Dragonscale Bush (*Atriplex draconis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Dominant shrub in semi-arid zones of Vorth Empire
- **Characteristics:** Silver-gray leaves with scale-like texture, extremely salt-tolerant
- **Height:** 0.5-1.5 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Leaves eaten by Vorthic people during droughts (edible when cooked)
- **Ecology:** Accumulates salts from soil, eventually kills itself
- **Coverage:** 20-40% ground cover
