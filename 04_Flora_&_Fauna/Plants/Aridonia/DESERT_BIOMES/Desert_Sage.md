# Desert Sage (*Salvia desertorum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Aroma: Powerful, resinous
- Flowers: Purple spikes, attract rare desert pollinators
- Uses: Medicinal (antiseptic), culinary, ceremonial
- Adaptation: Thick cuticle, aromatic oils reduce herbivory
