# Sandworm Cactus (*Echinocactus vermis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Deep desert; can survive 10+ years without rain
- **Characteristics:** Barrel-shaped, no visible spines (internal), stores vast water
- **Magical Properties:** Pulp grants heat resistance for 8 hours
- **Height:** 0.5-2 meters
- **Diameter:** 0.3-1 meter
- **Cultural Use:** Emergency water source; eating pulp is last resort
- **Danger:** Contains toxic alkaloids in outer layers
