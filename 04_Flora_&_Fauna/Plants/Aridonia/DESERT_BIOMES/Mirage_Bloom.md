# Mirage Bloom (*Datura illusio*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Scattered throughout deep desert
- **Characteristics:** Grows seemingly without water, large trumpet flowers
- **Magical Properties:** Flowers cause powerful hallucinations; used by shamans
- **Danger Level:** Extreme - all parts highly toxic
- **Height:** 0.3-1 meter
- **Cultural Use:** Vorthic shamans use carefully for vision quests
- **Ecology:** Pollinated by nocturnal sphinx moths
