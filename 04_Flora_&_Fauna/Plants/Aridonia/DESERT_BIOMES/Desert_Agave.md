# Desert Agave (*Agave deserti*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Form: Large rosette, up to 2 meters across
- Lifecycle: Grows 10-30 years, flowers once, dies
- Flower Stalk: 2-5 meters tall, edible when young
- Uses: Heart (roasted), fiber from leaves, fermented drink
- Cultural: Harvesting is communal activity with ritual significance
