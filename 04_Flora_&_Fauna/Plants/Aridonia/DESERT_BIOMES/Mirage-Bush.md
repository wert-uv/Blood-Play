# Mirage-Bush (*Fouquieria phantasma*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Rocky slopes, washes
- **Height:** 2-4 meters
- **Form:** Multiple whip-like stems from base
- **Leaves:** Tiny, appear only after rain, drop in drought
- **Flowers:** Tubular, orange-red, at stem tips
- **Unique Property:** Appears to shimmer in heat (creates mirages)
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Completely leafless most of the year

### Additional Info
- Living fences (spiny stems)
- Flower nectar attracts hummingbirds (hunted)
- Stems used as walking sticks
