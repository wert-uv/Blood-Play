# Sun Moss (*Syntrichia caninervis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Part of biological soil crust
- **Characteristics:** Tiny moss that can survive in complete dormancy
- **Magical Properties:** When rehydrated, briefly glows with stored sunlight
- **Ecology:** Active only during dew or rare rains
