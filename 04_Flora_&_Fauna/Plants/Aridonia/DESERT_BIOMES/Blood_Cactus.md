# Blood Cactus (*Opuntia sanguineus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Rocky deserts, not sand dunes
- **Height:** 1-3 meters
- **Form:** Branching pads, thick and fleshy
- **Spines:** 5-8cm, red-tinged
- **Flowers:** Blood-red, bloom after rare rains
- **Fruit:** Edible but sour
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** CAM photosynthesis, stores water for years

### Additional Info
- Emergency water source (pads contain moisture)
- Fruit: made into preserves
- Folk medicine: anti-inflammatory
- Symbol of resilience in desert cultures
