# Brittlebush (*Encelia farinosa*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Leaves: Silver-gray, covered in reflective hairs
- Flowers: Yellow daisies on tall stalks
- Adaptation: Drops leaves in drought, goes dormant
- Resin: Aromatic, used as incense
- Ecology: Dominates bajadas (alluvial fan slopes)
