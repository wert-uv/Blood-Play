# Cholla Cactus (*Cylindropuntia bigelovii*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Common Name: "Jumping cholla" (segments detach easily)
- Spines: Barbed, extremely difficult to remove
- Ecology: Provides crucial shelter for small desert animals
- Danger: Painful to encounter, avoided by large animals
