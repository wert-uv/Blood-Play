# Blackbrush (*Coleogyne ramosissima*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Form: Dense, thorny, dark-branched shrub
- Ecology: Long-lived (200+ years), slow growing
- Indicator: Presence indicates ancient, undisturbed desert
- Uses: Extremely hard wood, tool handles
