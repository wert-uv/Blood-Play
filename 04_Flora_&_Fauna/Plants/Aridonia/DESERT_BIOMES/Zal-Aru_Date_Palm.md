# Zal-Aru Date Palm (*Phoenix zalarensis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** All permanent oases, cultivated
- **Height:** 15-25 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 40-60cm
- **Fronds:** Pinnate, 3-5 meters long
- **Dates:** Large, sweet, main calorie source
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Requires permanent water, tolerates brackish

### Additional Info
- Dates: dried, pressed into cakes, fermented
- Fronds: roofing, baskets, fans
- Trunk: construction timber
- Sap: palm wine
- ABSOLUTELY CRITICAL to oasis survival
