# Water Reed (*Phragmites aquaticus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Oasis margins, any permanent water
- **Height:** 2-4 meters
- **Form:** Bamboo-like stems, feathery seed heads
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Wetland obligate, spreads aggressively

### Additional Info
- Building material (bundled reeds)
- Thatch, mats, screens
- Young shoots: edible vegetable
- Indicators of permanent water
