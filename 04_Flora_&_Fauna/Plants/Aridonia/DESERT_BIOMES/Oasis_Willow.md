# Oasis Willow (*Salix oasis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Larger oases only
- **Characteristics:** Drooping branches, requires permanent water
- **Height:** 8-15 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Shade trees, wood for tools
- **Ecology:** Indicates reliable permanent water
