# Dragon's Tongue (*Yucca draconis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** Throughout desert, especially near oases
- **Height:** 2-5 meters
- **Form:** Rosette of sword-like leaves, woody trunk
- **Leaves:** Rigid, pointed, fibrous
- **Flowers:** Tall stalk (3-5m), creamy white bells
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Only pollinates with specific moth species

### Additional Info
- Leaf fibers: rope, sandals, baskets
- Flower stalks: construction material
- Roots: soap substitute (saponins)
- Named for dragon-tooth appearance
