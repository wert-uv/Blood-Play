# Date Palm (*Phoenix draconis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES

- **Distribution:** All oases; primary food source for oasis settlements
- **Characteristics:** Produces nutritious fruit, requires groundwater
- **Height:** 15-25 meters
- **Lifespan:** 100-200 years
- **Cultural Use:** Entire tree used - fruit, wood, leaves for thatch, fiber for rope
- **Ecology:** Provides canopy that allows understory plants
