# Ocotillo (*Fouquieria splendens*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Form: Multiple tall, spiny stems from base
- Adaptation: Leafs out within days of rain, drops leaves in drought
- Flowers: Red tubular, at stem tips, hummingbird pollinated
- Uses: Living fences, flowers edible
- Unique: Can photosynthesize through green bark when leafless
