# Barrel Cactus (*Ferocactus cylindraceus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** DESERT_BIOMES


### Additional Info
- Form: Cylindrical, up to 1 meter tall
- Spines: Long, hooked, extremely sharp
- Water Storage: Can be tapped for emergency water (bitter)
- Flowers: Yellow-orange, crown of cactus
- Uses: Fruit edible, flesh can be cooked
