# Sea Rocket (*Cakile maritima*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Pink to white, four petals
- Adaptation: Fleshy leaves store water, tolerate salt spray
- Edible: Young leaves (spicy), high in vitamin C
- Ecology: Pioneer species on bare sand
