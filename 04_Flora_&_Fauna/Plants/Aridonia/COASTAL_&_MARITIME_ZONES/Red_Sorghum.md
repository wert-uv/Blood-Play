# Red Sorghum (*Sorghum vorthicum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Cultivated in semi-arid zones of Vorth Empire
- **Characteristics:** Drought-tolerant, reddish grain
- **Origin:** Domesticated by early Vorthic peoples
- **Yield:** Lower than wheat but grows in desert margins
- **Cultural Importance:** Essential for desert agriculture
