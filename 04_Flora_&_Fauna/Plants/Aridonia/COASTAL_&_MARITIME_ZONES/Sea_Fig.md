# Sea Fig (*Carpobrotus maritimus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Coastal dunes, rocky shores
- **Height:** 10-20cm, mat-forming
- **Leaves:** Succulent, triangular in cross-section
- **Flowers:** Brilliant magenta, open in sun
- **Fruit:** Edible, fig-like
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Salt-tolerant, forms extensive mats

### Additional Info
- Fruit: emergency food
- Leaves: poultice for burns
- Stabilizes coastal dunes
