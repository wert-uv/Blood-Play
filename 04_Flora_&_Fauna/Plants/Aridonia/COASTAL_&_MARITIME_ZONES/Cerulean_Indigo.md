# Cerulean Indigo (*Indigofera cerulea*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Cultivated on Cerulea and southern Westallis
- **Characteristics:** Deep blue dye from leaves
- **Cultural Significance:** Blue cloth = wealth and status
- **Origin:** Kingdom of Cerulea (gave kingdom its name)
- **Economic Importance:** Valuable dye; sumptuary laws restrict use to nobility
