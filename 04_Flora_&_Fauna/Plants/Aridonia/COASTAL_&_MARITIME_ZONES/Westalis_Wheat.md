# Westalis Wheat (*Triticum westalis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Cultivated throughout western and central regions
- **Characteristics:** Cold-hardy variety, yellow grain
- **Origin:** Domesticated in northern Westallis 3,000 years ago
- **Yield:** Foundation of bread-based diet
- **Cultural Importance:** Symbol of civilization vs wilderness
