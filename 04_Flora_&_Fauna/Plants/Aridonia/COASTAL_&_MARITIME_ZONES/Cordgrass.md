# Cordgrass (*Spartina alterniflora*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Adaptation: Salt-excreting glands, tolerates saltwater
- Ecology: Critical nursery habitat for fish
- Form: Tall (1-2m), spreads via rhizomes
- Uses: Thatching, nesting material for coastal peoples
