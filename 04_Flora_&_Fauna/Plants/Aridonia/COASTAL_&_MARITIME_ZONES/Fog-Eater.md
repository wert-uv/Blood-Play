# Fog-Eater (*Euphorbia nebulorum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Coastal fog zone only, within 20km of sea
- **Height:** 1-2 meters
- **Form:** Leafless, succulent columns
- **Sap:** Milky, highly toxic
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Obtains 80% of water from fog

### Additional Info
- Arrow poison (carefully prepared)
- Living fence (animals avoid it)
- Medicinal: external use only, extreme caution
