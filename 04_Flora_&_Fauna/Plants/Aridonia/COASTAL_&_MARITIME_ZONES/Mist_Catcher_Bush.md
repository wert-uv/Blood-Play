# Mist Catcher Bush (*Euphorbia nebula*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Coastal strip 0-15km inland
- **Characteristics:** Mesh of thin branches catches fog droplets
- **Height:** 1-2 meters
- **Ecology:** Primary water source from fog; adaptations to extract moisture
- **Cultural Use:** Shows where fog water can be harvested
