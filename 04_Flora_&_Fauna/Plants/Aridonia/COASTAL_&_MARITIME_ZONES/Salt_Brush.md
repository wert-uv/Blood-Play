# Salt Brush (*Atriplex marina*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Coastal dunes and salt flats
- **Characteristics:** Highly salt-tolerant, bluish-gray leaves
- **Height:** 0.5-1.5 meters
- **Ecology:** Stabilizes coastal sands
- **Cultural Use:** Indicator of salt-rich soils; avoided by farmers
