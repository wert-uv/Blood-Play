# Glasswort (*Salicornia europaea*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Form: Succulent, jointed stems, no leaves
- Color: Green to red (red in fall)
- Edible: Entire plant, salty taste, pickled or fresh
- Indicator: High salinity zones
