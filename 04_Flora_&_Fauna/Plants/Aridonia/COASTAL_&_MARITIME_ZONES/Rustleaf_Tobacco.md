# Rustleaf Tobacco (*Nicotiana rusticum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** COASTAL_&_MARITIME_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Major export crop from Rust and Debrica
- **Characteristics:** Leaves turn rust-red when cured
- **Origin:** Kingdom of Rust (gave kingdom its name)
- **Uses:** Smoking, medicine (mild painkiller)
- **Economic Importance:** Major trade good; "leaf wars" fought over monopolies
