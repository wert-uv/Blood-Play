# Bloodroot (*Sanguinaria verdantis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Root: Bright red sap when cut
- Flowers: White, early spring bloomer
- Uses: Red dye, medicinal (respiratory), small doses only (toxic)
- Cultural: Used in warrior initiation ceremonies
**Trillium clusters** - Spring ephemerals, three-petaled flowers
**Sword fern** - Evergreen, forms dense colonies
**Wood sorrel** - Heart-shaped leaves, edible, sour taste
**Mushroom diversity** - 200+ species, including bioluminescent varieties
