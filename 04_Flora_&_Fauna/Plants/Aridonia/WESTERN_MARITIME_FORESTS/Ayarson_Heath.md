# Ayarson Heath (*Erica montanus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Mountain slopes, particularly around Ayarson foothills
- **Height:** 0.5-1.5 meters
- **Flowers:** Bell-shaped, purple-pink, bloom in masses
- **Leaves:** Needle-like, evergreen
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Thrives on poor, acidic soils

### Additional Info
- Honey production (Ayarson mountain honey prized)
- Decorative gardens
- Brewing (adds bitter, floral notes)
