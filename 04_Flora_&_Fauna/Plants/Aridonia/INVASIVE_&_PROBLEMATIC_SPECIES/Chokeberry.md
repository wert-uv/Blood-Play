# Chokeberry (*Strangulex vitis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** INVASIVE_&_PROBLEMATIC_SPECIES


### Additional Info
- Growth: Smothers other vegetation
- Spread: Birds eat berries, distribute seeds widely
- Problem: Difficult to eradicate (deep roots)
- Uses: None (fruit inedible to humans)
