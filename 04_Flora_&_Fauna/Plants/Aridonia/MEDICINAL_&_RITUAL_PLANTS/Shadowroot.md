# Shadowroot (*Mandragora nocturna*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MEDICINAL_&_RITUAL_PLANTS


### Additional Info
- Appearance: Human-shaped root
- Folklore: Screams when pulled from ground
- Uses: Powerful sedative, pain relief, poisons
- Danger: Highly toxic in wrong doses
- Harvesting: Elaborate rituals to harvest safely
