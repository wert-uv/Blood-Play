# Woundwort (*Stachys officinalis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** MEDICINAL_&_RITUAL_PLANTS


### Additional Info
- Uses: Stops bleeding, prevents infection
- Form: Poultice or powder on wounds
- Cultural: Carried by soldiers, travelers
