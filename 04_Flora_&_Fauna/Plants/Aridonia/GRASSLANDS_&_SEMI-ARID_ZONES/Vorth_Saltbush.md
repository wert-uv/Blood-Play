# Vorth Saltbush (*Atriplex vorthii*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Dry lake beds, saline areas
- **Height:** 0.5-1 meter
- **Leaves:** Gray-scaly, salty to taste
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Salt-accumulator, improves saline soils

### Additional Info
- Livestock browse (accumulates salts, animals seek it)
- Indicator of saline soils
- Edible young leaves (salty green)
