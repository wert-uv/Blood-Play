# Sagebrush

*Artemisia argentea*
Biome: GRASSLANDS & SEMI-ARID ZONES
Sub-Biome: RAIN SHADOW TRANSITION (Eastern Westallis, Western Vorth Empire borders)

- Height: 0.5-2 meters, woody base
- Aroma: Strong, distinctive, aromatic oils
- Adaptation: Silver leaves reflect heat, reduce water loss
- Ecology: Allelopathic (inhibits other plants)
- Uses: Ceremonial smudging, medicinal tea, insect repellent
- Cultural: Sacred plant, used in cleansing rituals
