# Yucca (*Yucca glauca*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Form: Basal rosette of stiff, sword-like leaves
- Flowers: Tall stalk with white bell flowers
- Ecology: Obligate mutualism with yucca moth (only pollinator)
- Uses: Fiber from leaves (rope, sandals), roots for soap
- Adaptation: Waxy leaves prevent water loss
