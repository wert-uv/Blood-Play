# Rabbitbrush (*Chrysothamnus nauseosus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Bright yellow, late summer (when little else blooms)
- Ecology: Important late-season nectar source
- Uses: Yellow dye, medicinal (respiratory)
- Adaptation: Deep taproot, drought resistant
