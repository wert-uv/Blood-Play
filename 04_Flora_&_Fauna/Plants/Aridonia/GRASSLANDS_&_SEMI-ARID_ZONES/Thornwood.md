# Thornwood (*Acacia vortica*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Scattered throughout transition zone
- **Height:** 3-6 meters
- **Thorns:** Paired, straight, 3-5cm long
- **Leaves:** Bipinnate, small leaflets
- **Flowers:** Spherical yellow puffballs
- **Pods:** Flat, twisted, 10-15cm
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Nitrogen-fixing, provides shade for other plants

### Additional Info
- Pods: emergency livestock feed
- Wood: extremely hard, tool handles
- Gum arabic extracted from bark
