# Speargrass (*Stipa hastilis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 0.5-1 meter
- Seeds: Long awned, drill into soil and animal fur
- Ecology: Fire-adapted, spreads after burns
- Uses: Thatching, basket weaving (when green)
- Danger: Seeds can injure livestock, penetrate skin
