# Needlegrass (*Stipa tennuissima*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Extensive grasslands in transition zone
- **Height:** 30-60cm
- **Form:** Fine, hair-like leaves
- **Seeds:** Long awns (10cm), spiral-shaped
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Deep roots, drought dormancy

### Additional Info
- Seasonal grazing
- Seeds dangerous to livestock (penetrate skin)
- Decorative: dried for arrangements
