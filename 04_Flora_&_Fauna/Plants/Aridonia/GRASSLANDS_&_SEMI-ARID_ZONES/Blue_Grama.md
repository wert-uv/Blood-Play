# Blue Grama (*Bouteloua caeruleus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 20-40 cm, forms short turf
- Adaptation: Deep roots (2+ meters), drought resistant
- Ecology: Critical grazing species, prevents erosion
- Quality: Nutritious forage, cures well for winter
