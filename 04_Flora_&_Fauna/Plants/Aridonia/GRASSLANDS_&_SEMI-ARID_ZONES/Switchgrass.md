# Switchgrass (*Panicum virgatum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 1-2 meters, tall and stately
- Ecology: Provides cover for ground-nesting birds
- Uses: Thatching, fiber, erosion control
- Fire: Quickly regenerates after burns
