# Prickly Pear Cactus (*Opuntia polyacantha*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Form: Flat pads covered in spines
- Flowers: Yellow to pink, large and showy
- Fruit: Edible (carefully remove spines)
- Adaptation: CAM photosynthesis, stores water in pads
- Uses: Emergency water source, fruit (nopales), pads edible when cooked
