# Sage of Sert (*Artemisia sertensis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES

- **Distribution:** Throughout semi-arid zone, very common
- **Height:** 0.5-1.5 meters
- **Form:** Multi-branched shrub
- **Leaves:** Small, gray-green, aromatic (camphor-like)
- **Flowers:** Tiny, yellow, in spikes
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Deep taproot (5-10m), allelopathic

### Additional Info
- Vorth Empire incense (temples, ceremonies)
- Medicinal: digestive aid, antimicrobial
- Fuel source
