# Prairie Coneflower (*Ratibida columnifera*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** GRASSLANDS_&_SEMI-ARID_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Drooping yellow petals, tall brown cone
- Height: 0.5-1 meter
- Uses: Medicinal tea (pain relief), ornamental
- Ecology: Deep taproot, survives drought and fire
