## **Earthquake**

_Level 8 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 500 ft
- MPCost:: 180 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: The ground shakes violently in a massive radius.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: 100ft radius intense tremor. Creatures take 5d10 damage, knocked prone. Structures collapse.
- SecondaryEffects:: Creates fissures (10ft wide, 40ft deep). Difficult terrain for duration.
