## **Grease**

_Level 1 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 25 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: Slick grease covers the ground in a 10-foot square.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates 10ft square of difficult terrain. Creatures entering must make Reflex save DC 13 or fall prone.
- SecondaryEffects:: Flammable - can ignite for `dice: 2d4` fire damage to creatures in area.
