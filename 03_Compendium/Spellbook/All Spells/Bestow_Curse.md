## **Bestow Curse**

_Level 3 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 70 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You curse a creature with persistent misfortune.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Choose: disadvantage on one stat's checks/saves, disadvantage on attacks against you, must save or waste action, or take 1d8 necrotic per turn.
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 16 to resist. Can be made permanent with higher MP cost (200 MP).
