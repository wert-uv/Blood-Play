## **Omniscience**

_Level 9 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: Self
- MPCost:: 250 MP
- Duration:: 1 Turn

---

Description:: For a brief moment, you touch the infinite well of universal knowledge.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: For 1 turn, you know the answer to any question about current reality (not future/past).
- SecondaryEffects:: Can ask 3 questions. Answers are always truthful and complete. Afterward, suffer -4 INT for 24 hours.
