## **Speak with Dead**

_Level 3 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 60 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You grant a corpse semblance of life to answer questions.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Ask corpse up to 5 questions. It answers based on knowledge in life.
- SecondaryEffects:: Corpse isn't obligated to be truthful. Cannot use on same corpse for 10 days.
