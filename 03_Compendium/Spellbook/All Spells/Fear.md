## **Fear**

_Level 3 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self (30 ft cone)
- MPCost:: 70 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You project a phantasmal image of each creature's worst fears.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures in cone frightened, must flee from you. Will save DC 16 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can attempt Will save DC 16 at end of each turn when can't see you.
