## **Freedom of Movement**

_Level 4 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 70 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You touch a willing creature, granting freedom from magical and physical restraints.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Movement unaffected by difficult terrain, spells, paralysis, or being underwater.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can spend 5ft movement to escape grapples or restraints automatically.
