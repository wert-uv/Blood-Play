## **Conjure Animals**

_Level 3 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 75 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You summon fey spirits in animal form.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Summon beasts: 1 CR 2, 2 CR 1, 4 CR 1/2, or 8 CR 1/4. They obey your commands.
- SecondaryEffects:: Each has stats appropriate to CR. Disappear when reduced to 0 HP or spell ends.
