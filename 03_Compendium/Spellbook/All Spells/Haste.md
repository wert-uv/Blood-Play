## **Haste**

_Level 3 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 60 MP
- Duration:: 5 Turns

---

Description:: Time bends around the target, granting supernatural speed.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target gains 1 additional action per turn and +4 to Agility and Reflex.
- SecondaryEffects:: When spell ends, target cannot move or take actions for 1 turn (exhaustion).
