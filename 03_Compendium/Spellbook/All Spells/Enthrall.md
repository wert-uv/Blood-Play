## **Enthrall**

_Level 2 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 40 MP
- Duration:: 1 Minute

---

Description:: You weave a distracting string of words, capturing attention.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures who can hear you have disadvantage on Perception checks to notice others.
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 14 to resist. Hostile creatures have advantage on save.
