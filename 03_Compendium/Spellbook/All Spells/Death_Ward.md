## **Death Ward**

_Level 4 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 75 MP
- Duration:: 8 Hours

---

Description:: You touch a creature and grant it protection from death.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: First time target would drop to 0 HP, they instead drop to 1 HP and spell ends.
- SecondaryEffects:: Also prevents instant death effects.
