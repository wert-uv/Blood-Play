## **Feeblemind**

_Level 8 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 150 ft
- MPCost:: 180 MP
- Duration:: 30 Days

---

Description:: You shatter a creature's intellect and personality.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target's INT and Will reduced to 1. Cannot cast spells, use abilities, or communicate coherently.
- SecondaryEffects:: INT save DC 21 to resist. Can attempt INT save every 30 days to recover.
