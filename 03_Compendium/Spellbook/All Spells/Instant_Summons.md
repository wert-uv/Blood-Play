## **Instant Summons**

_Level 6 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Minute
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 130 MP
- Duration:: Until used

---

Description:: You touch an object and create a mystic connection to it.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Touch item (max 10 lbs). At any time after, can crush sapphire focus to instantly summon item to your hand.
- SecondaryEffects:: No range limit (same plane). Item teleports through obstacles.
