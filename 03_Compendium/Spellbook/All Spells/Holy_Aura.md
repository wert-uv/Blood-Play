## **Holy Aura**

_Level 8 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self (30 ft radius)
- MPCost:: 210 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: Divine light emanates from you, protecting allies.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Allies in range emit dim light, advantage on all saves, others have disadvantage attacking them.
- SecondaryEffects:: Fiends and undead attacking affected creatures must make RES save DC 21 or be blinded.
