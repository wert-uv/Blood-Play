## **Cloud of Daggers**

_Level 2 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 40 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You fill the air with spinning daggers in a 5-foot cube.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures starting turn in cube or entering it take `dice: 4d4` slashing damage.
- SecondaryEffects:: Cube remains stationary. No save - automatic damage.
