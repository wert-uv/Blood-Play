## **Cone of Cold**

_Level 5 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self (60 ft cone)
- MPCost:: 110 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: A blast of cold air erupts from your hands.

---
