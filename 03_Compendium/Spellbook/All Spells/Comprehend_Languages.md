## **Comprehend Languages**

_Level 1 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self
- MPCost:: 25 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You gain the ability to understand any spoken or written language.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Can understand (but not speak) any language heard or read.
- SecondaryEffects:: Useful for ancient texts, foreign tongues, or coded messages.
