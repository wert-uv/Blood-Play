## **Slow**

_Level 3 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 60 MP
- Duration:: 5 Turns

---

Description:: You trap targets in sluggish time.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Up to 6 creatures within 40ft cube have their speed halved and -4 to Agility/Reflex.
- SecondaryEffects:: Affected creatures can only take 1 action OR 1 bonus action per turn, not both.
