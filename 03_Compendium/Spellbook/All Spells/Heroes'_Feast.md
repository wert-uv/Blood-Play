## **Heroes' Feast**

_Level 6 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 140 MP
- Duration:: 24 Hours

---

Description:: You conjure a magnificent feast that grants blessings to all who partake.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Up to 12 creatures who eat gain: cure of all diseases/poison, immunity to poison/fright, advantage on Will saves, +20 max HP.
- SecondaryEffects:: Benefits last 24 hours. Feast takes 1 hour to consume.
