## **Mold Earth**

_Level 1 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Non-Verbal
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 10 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: You manipulate loose earth and stone.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Move up to 5 cubic feet of dirt, creating holes, mounds, or shapes.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can create difficult terrain or simple earthen shapes.
