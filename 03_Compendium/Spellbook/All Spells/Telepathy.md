## **Telepathy**

_Level 7 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Unlimited (same plane)
- MPCost:: 140 MP
- Duration:: 24 Hours

---

Description:: You create a telepathic link with a willing creature.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Can communicate telepathically with one creature you know, regardless of distance (same plane).
- SecondaryEffects:: Can share images, emotions, and basic sensory information.
