## **Antipathy/Sympathy**

_Level 7 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Hour
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 160 MP
- Duration:: 10 Days

---

Description:: You cause an object or location to repel or attract creatures.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Choose creature type and Antipathy (repels) or Sympathy (attracts).
- SecondaryEffects:: Antipathy: creatures frightened, must flee. Sympathy: creatures drawn to stay. Will save DC 20 to resist.
