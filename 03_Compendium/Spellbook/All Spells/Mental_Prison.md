## **Mental Prison**

_Level 6 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 140 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You trap a creature in an illusory cell only it can perceive.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target restrained, takes `dice: 5d10` psychic damage initially and each turn. INT save DC 19 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: If moves more than 10ft from starting position, takes `dice: 10d10` psychic and spell ends.
