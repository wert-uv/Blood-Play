## **Mirror Image**

_Level 2 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Non-Verbal
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self
- MPCost:: 45 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: Three illusory duplicates of yourself appear.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates 3 duplicate images. When attacked, roll 1d4 (1=hits you, 2-4=hits duplicate).
- SecondaryEffects:: Each duplicate destroyed when hit. Duplicates mimic your movements.
