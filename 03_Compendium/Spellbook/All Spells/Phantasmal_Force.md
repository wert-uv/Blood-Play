## **Phantasmal Force**

_Level 2 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 45 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You craft an illusion in a creature's mind.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target perceives illusion as real (object, creature, or phenomenon in 10ft cube). INT save DC 15 to disbelieve.
- SecondaryEffects:: Target rationalizes illogical outcomes. Can deal `dice: 1d6` psychic damage per turn if illusion could harm them.
