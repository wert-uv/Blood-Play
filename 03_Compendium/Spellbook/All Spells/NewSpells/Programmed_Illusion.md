## **Programmed Illusion**

_Level 6 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: 120 ft
- MPCost:: 130 MP
- Duration:: Until dispelled

---

Description:: You create an illusion that activates when specific conditions are met.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Create illusion (30ft cube) with trigger condition. When triggered, plays out for 5 turns.
- SecondaryEffects:: Investigation check DC 19 to determine it's an illusion. Includes sight, sound, smell, temperature.
