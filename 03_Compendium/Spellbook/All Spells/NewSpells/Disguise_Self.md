## **Disguise Self**

_Level 1 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self
- MPCost:: 25 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You make yourself look different.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Change appearance including clothing, armor, weapons, and physical features. Must keep same basic shape.
- SecondaryEffects:: Investigation check DC 14 to see through disguise. Physical inspection reveals illusion.
