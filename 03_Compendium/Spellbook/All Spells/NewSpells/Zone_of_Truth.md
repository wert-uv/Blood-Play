## **Zone of Truth**

_Level 2 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 50 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You create a zone where lies cannot be spoken.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures in 15ft radius sphere cannot deliberately lie. Will save DC 15 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: You know who succeeded/failed the save. Can remain silent or be evasive.
