## **Locate Creature**

_Level 4 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 1000 ft
- MPCost:: 70 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You sense the direction to a specific creature you describe.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Sense direction to nearest creature of described type within 1000ft.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can track moving creatures. Blocked by running water or lead.
