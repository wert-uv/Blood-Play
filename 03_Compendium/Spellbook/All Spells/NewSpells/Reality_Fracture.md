## **Reality Fracture**

_Level 9 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 120 ft
- MPCost:: 260 MP
- Duration:: 5 Turns

---

Description:: You shatter the boundary between illusion and reality.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Create illusion (60ft cube). For duration, the illusion becomes semi-real - deals actual damage, creates actual effects.
- SecondaryEffects:: Illusory damage/effects are 75% real. Investigation check DC 23 reduces effectiveness to 50%. Persists even after identified.
