## **Clone**

_Level 8 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Hour
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 190 MP
- Duration:: Permanent

---

Description:: You create an inert duplicate of a living creature.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Create duplicate body. If original dies, soul transfers to clone and awakens there.
- SecondaryEffects:: Clone matures in 120 days. Can be younger version. Needs flesh sample.
