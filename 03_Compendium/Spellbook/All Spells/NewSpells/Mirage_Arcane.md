## **Mirage Arcane**

_Level 7 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: Sight
- MPCost:: 170 MP
- Duration:: 10 Days

---

Description:: You make terrain in a 1-mile square look, sound, smell, and even feel like another type of terrain.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Transform appearance of terrain completely. Changes feel real to touch.
- SecondaryEffects:: Structures, equipment, and creatures not changed. Investigation check DC 20 to see through.
