## **Locate Object**

_Level 2 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 1000 ft
- MPCost:: 40 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You sense the direction to a specific object you describe.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: If object is within 1000ft, you sense its direction (but not distance).
- SecondaryEffects:: Cannot locate if object is blocked by lead or moving faster than you.
