## **Hypnotic Pattern**

_Level 3 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 120 ft
- MPCost:: 65 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You create a twisting pattern of colors that mesmerizes those who see it.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures in 30ft cube become charmed and incapacitated, staring at pattern. Will save DC 16 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: Effect ends on individual if they take damage or someone uses action to shake them.
