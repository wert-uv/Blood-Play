## **Gaseous Form**

_Level 2 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 50 MP
- Duration:: 30 Turns

---

Description:: You transform a willing creature into a misty cloud.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target becomes cloud of gas, can fly 10ft per turn, pass through small openings.
- SecondaryEffects:: Resistant to physical damage, cannot attack or cast most spells.
