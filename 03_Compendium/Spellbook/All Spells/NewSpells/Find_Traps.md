## **Find Traps**

_Level 1 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 120 ft
- MPCost:: 30 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: You sense the presence of traps and hazards.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Reveals presence (but not location) of traps within range.
- SecondaryEffects:: Cannot detect natural hazards, only deliberate traps.
