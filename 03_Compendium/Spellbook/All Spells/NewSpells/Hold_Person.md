## **Hold Person**

_Level 2 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 50 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You paralyze a humanoid with magical force.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target humanoid is paralyzed. Will save DC 15 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can attempt save at end of each turn. Attacks against paralyzed creature auto-crit.
