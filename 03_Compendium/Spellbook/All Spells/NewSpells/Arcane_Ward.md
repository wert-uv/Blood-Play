## **Arcane Ward**
(Abjuration/Protection)Covers all magic used to protect, banish, or create barriers.


_Level 1 Aegis_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 30 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You weave protective magic around a creature, forming an invisible barrier.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates a ward with 30 HP that absorbs damage before affecting the protected creature.
- SecondaryEffects:: Ward regenerates 5 HP per turn if not damaged that turn.
