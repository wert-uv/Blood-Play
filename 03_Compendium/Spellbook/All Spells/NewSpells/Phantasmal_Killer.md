## **Phantasmal Killer**

_Level 4 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 120 ft
- MPCost:: 95 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You tap into a creature's nightmares to create an illusion only it can see.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target sees its worst fear. Will save DC 17 or frightened and takes `dice: 4d10` psychic damage each turn.
- SecondaryEffects:: Repeat save at end of each turn. Half damage on successful save.
