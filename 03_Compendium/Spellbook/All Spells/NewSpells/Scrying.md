## **Scrying**

_Level 5 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: Unlimited
- MPCost:: 120 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You spy on a creature from any distance.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Create invisible sensor near target creature. Can see and hear through it.
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 16 to resist. Modifier based on knowledge of target (+5 secondhand/-5 possession).
