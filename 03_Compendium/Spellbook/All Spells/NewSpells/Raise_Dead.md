## **Raise Dead**

_Level 5 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Hour
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 120 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: You return a dead creature to life.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creature dead for up to 10 days returns to life with 1 HP.
- SecondaryEffects:: Penalty: -4 to all rolls until 4 long rests completed. Cannot raise creature that died of old age.
