## **Confusion**

_Level 4 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 90 ft
- MPCost:: 85 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You assault creatures' minds with contradictory impulses.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures in 10ft radius act randomly each turn (1d4: 1=attack self, 2-3=do nothing, 4=attack random creature).
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 17 to resist. Can attempt save at end of each turn.
