## **Calm Emotions**

_Level 2 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 40 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You attempt to suppress strong emotions in a group.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures in 20ft sphere lose charm/frighten effects OR become indifferent (not hostile).
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 15 to resist. Effects suppressed, not removed.
