## **Prismatic Wall**

_Level 9 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 240 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: A shimmering wall of seven distinct layers springs into existence.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates wall (90ft long, 30ft high, 1 inch thick). Seven layers, each with different effect and must be destroyed in order.
- SecondaryEffects:: Layers: Red (Fire), Orange (Acid), Yellow (Lightning), Green (Poison), Blue (Cold), Indigo (Restrains), Violet (Banishes). Each layer has unique removal condition.
