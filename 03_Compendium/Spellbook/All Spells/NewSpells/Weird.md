## **Weird**

_Level 9 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 120 ft
- MPCost:: 220 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You draw on nightmares of multiple creatures, creating illusions visible only to them.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: All creatures in 30ft sphere see their worst nightmares. Will save DC 22 or frightened and take `dice: 4d10` psychic each turn.
- SecondaryEffects:: Repeat save at end of each turn. On successful save, spell ends for that creature.
