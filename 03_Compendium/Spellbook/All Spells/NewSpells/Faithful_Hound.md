## **Faithful Hound**

_Level 4 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 85 MP
- Duration:: 8 Hours

---

Description:: You conjure a phantom watchdog.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates invisible hound that guards location. Barks if Small or larger creature approaches within 30ft.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can attack one creature per turn for `dice: 4d8` piercing damage if you command it.
