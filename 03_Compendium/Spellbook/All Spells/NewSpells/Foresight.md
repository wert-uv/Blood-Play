## **Foresight**

_Level 9 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Minute
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 210 MP
- Duration:: 8 Hours

---

Description:: You gain supernatural awareness of danger and opportunity.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Cannot be surprised, advantage on all rolls, attackers have disadvantage against you.
- SecondaryEffects:: Receive cryptic warnings about imminent dangers 6 seconds before they occur.
