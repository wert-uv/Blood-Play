## **Mass Hallucination**

_Level 9 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Minute
- Range:: 1 mile
- MPCost:: 240 MP
- Duration:: 30 Days

---

Description:: You create a massive shared hallucination affecting all creatures in range.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Create complex illusion up to 1 mile square. All creatures perceive it as real. Can include sight, sound, smell, temperature, and texture.
- SecondaryEffects:: INT save DC 22 to recognize as illusion. Even then, it still seems real to senses. Can change illusion using action while concentrating.
