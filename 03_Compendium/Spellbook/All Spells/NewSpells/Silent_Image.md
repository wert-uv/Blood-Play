## **Silent Image**

_Level 1 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 30 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You create the image of an object, creature, or phenomenon.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Create visual illusion no larger than 15ft cube. Can move illusion using action.
- SecondaryEffects:: Investigation check DC 14 to determine it's an illusion. No sound, smell, or other sensory elements.
