## **Water Walk**

_Level 2 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 30 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: The surface of water becomes solid under the target's feet.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Up to 10 creatures can walk on water, mud, snow, or quicksand as if solid ground.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can move at normal speed without sinking.
