## **Rope Trick**

_Level 2 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 45 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You touch a rope up to 60 feet long, causing one end to rise into the air.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates extradimensional space at top of rope. Can hold 8 Medium creatures.
- SecondaryEffects:: Space is invisible from outside. Creatures inside can't be detected or targeted from outside.
