## **Find Familiar**

_Level 1 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Hour
- Range:: 10 ft
- MPCost:: 40 MP
- Duration:: Until dismissed

---

Description:: You gain the service of a familiar, a spirit that takes animal form.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Summon familiar (bat, cat, crab, frog, hawk, lizard, octopus, owl, rat, raven, spider, or weasel).
- SecondaryEffects:: Can see through familiar's eyes, deliver touch spells through it. If dies, can resummon.
