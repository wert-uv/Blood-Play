## **Flaming Sphere**

_Level 2 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 50 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: A 5-foot-diameter sphere of fire appears and rolls where you direct.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Can move sphere 30ft per turn. Creatures within 5ft take fire damage. Reflex save DC 14 for half.
- SecondaryEffects:: Damage: `dice: 2d6` per turn. Ignites flammable objects.
