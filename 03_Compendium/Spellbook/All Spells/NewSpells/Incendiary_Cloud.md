## **Incendiary Cloud**

_Level 8 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 150 ft
- MPCost:: 190 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: A swirling cloud of smoke shot through with white-hot embers.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: 20ft radius cloud. Heavily obscures area. Creatures starting turn inside take `dice: 10d8` fire damage.
- SecondaryEffects:: Reflex save DC 21 for half. Cloud spreads around corners and is persistent.
