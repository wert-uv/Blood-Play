## **Revivify**

_Level 3 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 80 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: You touch a creature that has died within the last minute and return it to life.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creature returns to life with 1 HP.
- SecondaryEffects:: Cannot restore missing body parts. Doesn't work if died of old age.
