## **Transcendence**

_Level 9 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 320 MP
- Duration:: 24 Hours

---

Description:: You elevate a creature to a higher state of existence.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target gains immunity to all damage, cannot be affected by any spell or effect, can cast any spell without MP cost.
- SecondaryEffects:: After 24 hours, target must make RES save DC 20 or be exhausted for 7 days. Can only be cast on same target once per year.
