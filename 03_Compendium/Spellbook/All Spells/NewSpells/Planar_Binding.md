## **Planar Binding**

_Level 5 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Hour
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 150 MP
- Duration:: 24 Hours

---

Description:: You attempt to bind a celestial, elemental, fey, or fiend to your service.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Bind extraplanar creature to serve you for 24 hours. Will save DC 18 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can extend duration by using higher MP costs (7 days for 300 MP, 1 year for 600 MP).
