## **Clairvoyance**

_Level 3 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: 1 mile
- MPCost:: 70 MP

- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You create an invisible sensor in a location you know.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates sensor at known location. Can see or hear (choose one) through it.
- SecondaryEffects:: Sensor is invisible and immobile.
