## **Irresistible Dance**

_Level 6 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 130 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You force a creature to dance uncontrollably.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target must use all movement to dance in place. Disadvantage on all rolls.
- SecondaryEffects:: No initial save. Can attempt Will save DC 19 at end of each turn.
