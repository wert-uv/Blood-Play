## **Spirit Guardians**

_Level 3 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self (15 ft radius)
- MPCost:: 70 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: Spectral guardians flit around you, protecting you and striking foes.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Area is difficult terrain for enemies. Creatures starting turn or entering take `dice: 3d8` radiant or necrotic damage (your choice).
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 16 for half damage.
