## **Hold Monster**

_Level 5 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 90 ft
- MPCost:: 100 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You paralyze any creature with magical force.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target creature is paralyzed. Will save DC 18 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can target any creature type, not just humanoids. Can attempt save at end of each turn.
