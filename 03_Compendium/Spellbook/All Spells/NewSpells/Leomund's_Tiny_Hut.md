## **Leomund's Tiny Hut**

_Level 3 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Minute
- Range:: Self (10 ft radius)
- MPCost:: 55 MP
- Duration:: 8 Hours

---

Description:: A 10-foot-radius immobile dome of force springs into existence.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates dome. Interior comfortable (70°F). Creatures/objects inside can exit freely. Outside creatures can't enter.
- SecondaryEffects:: Opaque from outside, transparent from inside. Immune to all damage and spells.
