## **Sunburst**

_Level 7 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 150 ft
- MPCost:: 160 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: Brilliant sunlight flashes in a 60-foot radius.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: All creatures in radius take `dice: 12d6` radiant damage and blinded for 1 turn. Reflex save DC 20 for half damage and no blind.
- SecondaryEffects:: Triple damage to undead and oozes. Dispels magical darkness.
