## **Blur**

_Level 2 Phantasm_
The magic of tricks, shadows, and altering perception.

- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self
- MPCost:: 40 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: Your body becomes blurred and shifts constantly.

- SecondaryEffects:: Doesn't affect creatures with truesight or blindsight.
