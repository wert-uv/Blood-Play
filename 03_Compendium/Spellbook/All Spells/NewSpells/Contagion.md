## **Contagion**

_Level 5 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 100 MP
- Duration:: 7 Days

---

Description:: You inflict a disease on a creature.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target makes RES saves. After 3 failures, suffers disease for 7 days (Blinding Sickness, Filth Fever, Flesh Rot, Mindfire, Seizure, or Slimy Doom).
- SecondaryEffects:: Each disease has different debilitating effects. RES save DC 18.
