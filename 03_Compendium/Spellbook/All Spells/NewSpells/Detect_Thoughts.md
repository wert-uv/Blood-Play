## **Detect Thoughts**

_Level 1 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 35 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You probe the surface thoughts of nearby creatures.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Can read surface thoughts of creatures within range (what they're currently thinking).
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 14 to resist. Can probe deeper with another action.
