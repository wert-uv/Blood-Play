## **Solar Flare**

_Level 9 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 500 ft
- MPCost:: 280 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: You summon the power of a star, unleashing devastating radiant energy.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: 100ft radius. All creatures take `dice: 20d10` radiant damage. Reflex save DC 22 for half.
- SecondaryEffects:: Permanently blinds creatures that fail save. Disintegrates corpses and undead. Leaves crater of scorched earth.
