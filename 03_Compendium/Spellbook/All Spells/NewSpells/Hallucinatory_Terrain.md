## **Hallucinatory Terrain**

_Level 4 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 10 Minutes
- Range:: 300 ft
- MPCost:: 90 MP
- Duration:: 24 Hours

---

Description:: You make natural terrain look, sound, and smell like another type of terrain.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Make terrain in 150ft cube appear as different terrain type (forest to swamp, etc.).
- SecondaryEffects:: Investigation check DC 17 to see through. Tactical changes don't affect actual terrain.
