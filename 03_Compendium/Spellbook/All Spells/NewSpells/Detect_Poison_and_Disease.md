## **Detect Poison and Disease**

_Level 1 Vitality_

- CastingMethod:: Ritual
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self (30 ft radius)
- MPCost:: 20 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You sense the presence and location of poisons, poisonous creatures, and diseases.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Detect presence of poisons and diseases within 30ft.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can identify type of poison/disease with INT check DC 13.
