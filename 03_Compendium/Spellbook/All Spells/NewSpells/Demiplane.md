## **Demiplane**

_Level 8 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 200 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You create a shadowy door leading to a demiplane.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates door to your personal demiplane (30ft cube). Each casting can access same demiplane.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can create multiple separate demiplanes. Contents remain when spell ends.
