## **Chain Reaction**

_Level 6 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 150 ft
- MPCost:: 140 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: You trigger a cascading explosion that jumps between targets.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Choose element (Fire/Cold/Lightning). Primary target takes `dice: 12d6`, chains to all creatures within 15ft for `dice: 8d6`.
- SecondaryEffects:: Reflex save DC 19 for half. Secondary explosions chain once more at `dice: 4d6`.
