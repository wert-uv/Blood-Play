## **Reverse Gravity**

_Level 7 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 100 ft
- MPCost:: 140 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: Gravity reverses in a cylinder of space.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: All creatures and objects in 50ft radius, 100ft high cylinder fall upward.
- SecondaryEffects:: When spell ends, everything falls back down (10d6 damage if they hit ground).
