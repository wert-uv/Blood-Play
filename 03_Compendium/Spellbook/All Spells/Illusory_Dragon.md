## **Illusory Dragon**

_Level 8 Phantasm_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 120 ft
- MPCost:: 190 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You create a dragon of shadowy illusory energy.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Huge dragon illusion. As bonus action, can move 60ft and have it exhale (60ft cone, `dice: 7d6` damage of chosen type).
- SecondaryEffects:: Investigation check DC 21 to see through. Can make opportunity attacks (deals `dice: 2d6` damage).
