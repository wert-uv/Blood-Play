## **Arcane Eye**

_Level 3 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 60 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You create an invisible, magical eye that you can see through.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creates invisible floating eye you can control. Has normal and darkvision 30ft.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can move 30ft per turn in any direction, even through small openings.
