## **True Sight**

_Level 6 Cognition_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Touch
- MPCost:: 130 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You grant a creature the ability to see things as they truly are.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: See invisible creatures, see through illusions, detect shapechangers in true form, see into Ethereal Plane (60ft).
- SecondaryEffects:: Also grants darkvision 120ft.
