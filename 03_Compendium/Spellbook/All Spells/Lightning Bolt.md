## **Lightning Bolt**

_Level 3 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Non-Verbal
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: Self (100ft line)
- MPCost:: 60 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: A stroke of lightning forms a line 100ft long and 5ft wide. You release a powerful arc of electricity that blasts through multiple targets.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Deals lightning damage to all in line.
- SecondaryEffects:: Actual Damage is `10d10 + 9`.
