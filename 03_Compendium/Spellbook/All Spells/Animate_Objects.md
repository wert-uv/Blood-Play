(Transmutation/Change)Changing the physical properties of objects or creatures.

## **Animate Objects**

_Level 5 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 100 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: Objects spring to life at your command.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Animates up to 10 small objects or 5 medium objects to fight for you.
- SecondaryEffects:: Each has HP based on size, attacks with STR modifier. Follows simple commands.
