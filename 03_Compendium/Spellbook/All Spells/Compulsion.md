## **Compulsion**

_Level 4 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 80 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You force creatures to move in a direction of your choosing.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creatures in 30ft cube must use all movement to go in direction you choose on each turn.
- SecondaryEffects:: Will save DC 17 to resist. Can still take actions, just forced to move.
