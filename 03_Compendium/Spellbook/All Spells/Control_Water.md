## **Control Water**

_Level 4 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 150 ft
- MPCost:: 75 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You bend water to your will.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Control water in 100ft cube - part water, create whirlpool, redirect flow, or flood/drain.
- SecondaryEffects:: Affects rivers, lakes, oceans within range.
