## **Charm Person**

_Level 1 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 25 MP
- Duration:: 1 Hour

---

Description:: You magically influence a humanoid, making them regard you as a friendly acquaintance.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target regards you as friendly. Will save DC 14 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: Advantage on social checks with target. Ends if you or allies harm them.
