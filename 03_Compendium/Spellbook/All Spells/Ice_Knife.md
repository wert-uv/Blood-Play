## **Ice Knife**

_Level 1 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Non-Verbal
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 25 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: You create a shard of ice and hurl it at a creature.

---

- SecondaryEffects:: Piercing: `dice: 1d10`, Cold explosion: `dice: 2d6`. Reflex save DC 13 for half on explosion.
