## **Maze**

_Level 8 Summoning_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 60 ft
- MPCost:: 190 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: You banish a creature into a labyrinthine demiplane.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Creature vanishes, trapped in maze. Can attempt INT check DC 20 each turn to escape.
- SecondaryEffects:: Teleportation spells can also escape. No save to resist initial banishment.
