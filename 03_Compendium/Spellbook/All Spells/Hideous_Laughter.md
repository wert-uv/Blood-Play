## **Hideous Laughter**

_Level 1 Compulsion_

- CastingMethod:: Chanting
- CastingTime:: 1 Action
- Range:: 30 ft
- MPCost:: 35 MP
- Duration:: 10 Turns

---

Description:: A creature perceives everything as hilariously funny and falls into fits of laughter.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Target falls prone, incapacitated with laughter. Will save DC 14 to resist.
- SecondaryEffects:: Can attempt Will save DC 14 at end of each turn to end effect. Taking damage grants advantage on save.
