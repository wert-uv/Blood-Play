## **How to Use This Compendium**

Each spell entry includes:

- **Spell Name** and Level/Type
- **Available Classes** that can learn this spell
- **Casting Method, Time, Range, MP Cost, Duration**
- **Description and Mechanics**

**Note:** Multiple classes can learn the same spell where thematically appropriate. Some high-level spells are restricted to specific classes based on their unique magical traditions.
