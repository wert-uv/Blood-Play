# **ATTACKING SPELLS - DAMAGE FORMULAS**

_Only spells that deal direct damage_

---

## **FORMULA LEGEND**

- **IM** = Intelligence Modifier = floor(INT_Score / 4)
- **IS** = Intelligence Score
- **P** = Proficiency Level with spell type
- **SM** = Strength Modifier = floor(STR_Score / 4)
- **SS** = Strength Score

## **QUICK REFERENCE - DAMAGE BY LEVEL**

### **Cantrips (0)**

- Low: 1d8 + modifiers
- High: 1d10 + modifiers

### **Level 1**

- Average: 3d6 + modifiers
- High: 1d10 + modifiers (multiple hits)

### **Level 2**

- Average: 4d6 + modifiers
- High: 3 × (2d6 + modifiers)

### **Level 3**

- Average: 8d6 + modifiers
- Single target and area

### **Level 4**

- Average: 8-10d6/d8 + modifiers
- Chain effects common

### **Level 5**

- Average: 8d8 or dual element
- Multi-hit: 12d6+

### **Level 6-7**

- Average: 12-14d6 + modifiers
- Often includes special effects

### **Level 8**

- Average: 20d6 + modifiers
- Massive area effects

### **Level 9**

- Average: 20d10 or 16d6+ per turn
- Reality-breaking power

## **FORMULA PATTERNS**

### **Standard Progression**

- Level 0: (IM+P)dX + IS
- Level 1: (IM+P+1)dX + IS
- Level 2: (IM+P+2)dX + IS
- Level 3: (IM+P+4)dX + IS
- Level 4: (IM+P+5)dX + IS
- Level 5: (IM+P+4)d8 + IS
- Level 6: (IM+P+7)dX + IS
- Level 7: (IM+P+6)dX + IS
- Level 8: (IM+P+10)dX + IS
- Level 9: (IM+P+10)d10 + IS

### **Multi-Hit Spells**

- Each projectile/ray calculated separately
- Chain effects use diminishing formulas
- Area effects hit all targets once

### **Damage Over Time**

- Initial hit + per turn damage
- Often smaller die size but consistent
- Can stack with other effects
