# How To Describe Magic

Magic shouldn't just be "I cast Fireball." It should be an event.

**Step 1: The Senses**
Engage at least two senses.
*   **Sight:** The color, the shape, the brightness. (e.g., "A sphere of roaring orange flame.")
*   **Sound:** The crackle, the boom, the whisper. (e.g., "It hisses like a striking snake.")
*   **Smell:** Ozone, sulfur, burning wood, rain.
*   **Touch:** The heat, the chill, the static in the air.
*   **Taste:** Metallic, electric, bitter.

**Step 2: The Cast**
What does the caster do?
*   **Verbal:** A chant, a scream, a whisper.
*   **Somatic:** A gesture, a dance, a point.
*   **Material:** Crushing a gem, sprinkling sand, burning a leaf.

**Step 3: The Impact**
How does the world react?
*   **The Environment:** Does the grass wither? Does the water boil? Do the shadows lengthen?
*   **The Target:** Do they scream? Do they turn to ash? Do they freeze solid?

**Example:**
*   *Boring:* "The wizard casts Lightning Bolt."
*   *Better:* "The wizard chants a guttural word, and the air smells of ozone. arcs of blue electricity dance between his fingers before coalescing into a blinding white bolt that tears through the goblin, leaving only a smoking pair of boots."
