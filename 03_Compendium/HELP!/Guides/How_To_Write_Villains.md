# How To Write A Villain

**Step 1: The Motivation (The Why)**
A villain is the hero of their own story.
*   *Control:* "I must rule to bring order to chaos."
*   *Revenge:* "They destroyed my home, now I will destroy theirs."
*   *Survival:* "I do what I must to live."
*   *Ideology:* "Magic is dangerous and must be purged."

**Step 2: The Plan (The How)**
What are they actually doing?
*   *The Ritual:* Summoning a god/demon.
*   *The Conquest:* Invading a kingdom.
*   *The Corruption:* Turning leaders against each other.

**Step 3: The Presence**
How do the players know about them?
*   *Direct:* They meet the villain early (and survive).
*   *Indirect:* They see the aftermath of the villain's actions (burned villages).
*   *Rumors:* People whisper the villain's name in fear.

**Step 4: The Minions (The Lieutenants)**
The villain shouldn't be alone. Give them distinct underlings.
*   *The Brute:* The muscle.
*   *The Mage:* The brains.
*   *The Spy:* The eyes and ears.

**Step 5: The Flaw**
Every villain needs a weakness.
*   *Arrogance:* Underestimates the heroes.
*   *Obsession:* Blinded by their goal.
*   *Sentimentality:* Cannot harm a specific person.
