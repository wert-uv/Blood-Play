# How To Build Realistic Economies

**Step 1: The Resource (What do they have?)**
Every region needs something to trade.
*   *Iron/Minerals:* Boreas (implies mining, smithing).
*   *Food/Grain:* The Girdle (implies farming, fertility).
*   *Luxury/Silk:* Orynt (implies craftsmanship, wealth).

**Step 2: The Need (What do they lack?)**
*   The mining town needs food.
*   The farming town needs tools.
*   **Trade Route:** This creates roads, caravans, and bandit attacks.

**Step 3: The Currency**
Gold is standard, but flavor adds depth.
*   *Standard:* GP, SP, CP.
*   *Flavor:* "Dragons" (Gold), "Scales" (Silver), "Teeth" (Copper).
*   *Barter:* In wild areas, maybe they trade pelts or mana crystals.

**Step 4: Wealth Disparity**
*   **High:** Nobility live in palaces, peasants in mud huts. (e.g., Helica?)
*   **Low:** Everyone shares resources. (e.g., Sunder-Isle pirates?)
