# How To Create Distinct Cultures

**Step 1: The Values (What matters most?)**
*   *Honor/Martial Prowess:* "Death before dishonor." (e.g., Klingons/Spartans)
*   *Knowledge/Magic:* "The mind is the only weapon." (e.g., Wizards of Thay)
*   *Wealth/Trade:* "Everything has a price." (e.g., Ferengi/Venice)
*   *Community/Survival:* "The pack survives." (e.g., Nomads)

**Step 2: Social Norms & Taboos**
*   **Greeting:** A bow? A handshake? A spit?
*   **Taboo:** Is magic illegal? Is eating pork forbidden? Is showing your face shameful?

**Step 3: Holidays & Festivals**
*   *Harvest Festival:* Celebrating food.
*   *Day of the Dead:* Honoring ancestors.
*   *Founding Day:* Celebrating the nation.

**Step 4: Clothing & Cuisine**
*   **Clothing:** Robes (Helica), Furs (Boreas), Silks (Orynt).
*   **Food:** Spicy curries, bland stews, raw fish.
