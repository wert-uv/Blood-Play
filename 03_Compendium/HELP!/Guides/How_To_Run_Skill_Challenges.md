# How To Run Skill Challenges

A "Skill Challenge" is a montage scene where players use skills (Athletics, Persuasion, etc.) to achieve a goal.

**Step 1: The Goal**
What are they trying to do?
*   *Chase:* Catch the fleeing thief.
*   *Escape:* Outrun the lava flow.
*   *Research:* Find the hidden lore in the library.
*   *Social:* Convince the King to send aid.

**Step 2: The DC (Difficulty Class)**
*   **Easy:** DC 10
*   **Medium:** DC 15
*   **Hard:** DC 20

**Step 3: The Structure**
*   **Successes Required:** X (usually 3 for easy, 5 for medium, 7 for hard).
*   **Failures Allowed:** 3
*   If they get X successes before 3 failures, they win.

**Step 4: Running the Scene**
*   **Ask:** "What do you do?" and "What skill do you use?"
*   **Narrate:**
    *   *Player:* "I use Athletics to push the boulder."
    *   *Roll:* 18 (Success)
    *   *DM:* "You heave the rock aside, clearing the path for the others."
*   **Consequence (Failure):**
    *   *Player:* "I use History to find the symbol."
    *   *Roll:* 9 (Failure)
    *   *DM:* "You find the symbol, but you knock over a vase, alerting the guards. That's 1 failure."

**Step 5: The Outcome**
*   **Success:** They achieve the goal perfectly.
*   **Failure:** They achieve the goal but with a cost (lost HP, lost time, lost resources, or a new complication).
