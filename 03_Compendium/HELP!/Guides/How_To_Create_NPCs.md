# How To Create Memorable NPCs

**Step 1: The Core Concept**
Start with a simple archetype, but give it a twist.
*   *Archetype:* The grumpy blacksmith.
*   *Twist:* They are actually a pacifist former paladin who hates violence.

**Step 2: Distinctive Traits**
Give players something to remember them by. Pick **one** from each category:
*   **Visual:** A scar, a bright hat, a missing finger, impeccable posture.
*   **Auditory:** speak slowly, use a specific catchphrase ("By the blood!"), whisper, stutter.

**Step 3: What do they Want? (Motivation)**
Every NPC needs a goal, even if it's small.
*   *Immediate:* Sell these swords so I can buy lunch.
*   *Long-term:* Find an apprentice to pass on my skills.
*   *Secret:* Hide my past from the local lord.

**Step 4: The Relationship**
How do they react to the party?
*   *Friendly:* Helpful, offers discounts or info.
*   *Neutral:* Transactional, indifferent.
*   *Hostile:* Suspicious, rude, or actively working against them.

**Step 5: The Name**
*   Use a name generator or pick a sound that fits their race (e.g., Grunt = Orc, Flowing vowel = Elf).
*   Add a title or nickname (e.g., "Old One-Eye").
