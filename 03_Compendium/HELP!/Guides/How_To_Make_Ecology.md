# How To Make Realistic Ecologies

**Step 1: The Biome (Where are we?)**
*   *Forest:* High rainfall, dense vegetation, diverse life.
*   *Desert:* Low rainfall, hardy plants, nocturnal life.
*   *Underdark:* No sunlight, bioluminescence, fungi, strange gravity.

**Step 2: The Food Chain**
*   **Producers:** Plants/Fungi. (e.g., Magic Mushrooms, Ironwood Trees)
*   **Herbivores:** Deer, giant beetles, subterranean cows.
*   **Carnivores:** Wolves, spiders, roper.
*   **Apex Predators:** Dragons, Purple Worms.

**Step 3: Monster Niches**
Why is this monster here?
*   *Scavenger:* Eats carrion. (e.g., Carrion Crawler)
*   *Ambush:* Hides and waits. (e.g., Mimic)
*   *Hunter:* Actively pursues. (e.g., Owlbear)

**Step 4: Magical Influence**
Fantasy ecology is weird.
*   *Mana Leaks:* Mutated animals near magical sites.
*   *Elemental Portals:* Fire elementals living in a volcano.

**Step 5: The Human Element**
How do people survive here?
*   Do they farm giant mushrooms?
*   Do they hunt the dire rats?
*   Do they worship the local dragon?
