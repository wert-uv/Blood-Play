# How To Map Politics

**Step 1: The Rulers (Who is in charge?)**
*   **Monarchy:** King/Queen (Hereditary).
*   **Council:** Oligarchy (Wealthy/Elders).
*   **Theocracy:** Church (Faith-based).
*   **Democracy:** Elected officials (Rare in fantasy).

**Step 2: The Rivals (Who wants power?)**
Every ruler has enemies.
*   *Internal:* A rebellious duke, a jealous sibling.
*   *External:* A neighboring kingdom, an invading horde.
*   *Hidden:* A cult, a spy network.

**Step 3: The Leverage (How do they keep power?)**
*   **Military:** "I have the biggest army."
*   **Wealth:** "I pay everyone."
*   **Magic:** "I can incinerate you."
*   **Information:** "I know your secrets."

**Step 4: The Tensions**
*   *High Tension:* War is imminent.
*   *Low Tension:* Peace, but stagnation.
*   *Cold War:* Spies and assassinations.

**Step 5: How Players Fit In**
*   Do they need a favor from the King?
*   Are they hired by the rival Duke?
*   Do they accidentally expose the Spy?
