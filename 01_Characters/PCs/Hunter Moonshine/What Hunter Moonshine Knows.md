# Kingdom Of Rust 
Rust is located at Northern third of the west-central island containing Mixed temperate forest and coastal plains. Rust Occupies the northern coastal region, characterized by gently rolling forested hills transitioning to coastal lowlands. The northern coastline is relatively smooth with a few shallow bays. Dense deciduous and mixed forests cover approximately 70% of the territory, with cleared agricultural land radiating outward from the urban center. The capital settlement is positioned inland, roughly 5-7 kilometers from the northern coast, on a slight elevation that provides defensive advantage and fresh water access.
Rust kingdom was primarily a human kingdom but due to the increase in power of the eastern region of aridonia (Vorth empire and Westallis kingdom) Rust allied with and incorporated the small scattered settlements of hobgoblins and elves into the kingdom putting an end to the hobgoblin elf war.
Rust also allied with the Dwarven kingdom of ayaman forming the Eastern Union
to protect against any threats from the West.
After the inclusion of ayaman in the union Rust gained access to dwarven weapons, tools, armor and technology.
Rust is known for its iron deposits.

# Sprout Kingdom 
Sprout Kingdom is in North-central archipelago, between the western major island and the eastern continent it is Heavily forested island with limited cleared land. The Kingdom is mostly known for its diverse plant and animal life. And magical properties of said life along with Huge Trees. They have limited trade with mainland Aridonia and are a neutral faction for a most part as long as undisturbed but given the circumstances their advanced magical theory can prove terrifying. This kingdom is mostly inhabited by elves but they do take some stranglers if they wash ashore. These Stranglers have formed some of their own settlements.
God of the Beasts Vorgath is worshiped here primarily. 
# Kingdom Of Debrica
Debrica is Southern Interior of Western Mainland. Allied with the union against the East. Debrica is a common Trade Hub as it provides shelter and safe passage into the deep west. It is also one of the Richest Kingdoms with a Powerful Military. It is said Gold Flows like Water in the Kingdom. Debrica is a human Kingdom with a plethora of other species living alongside them. 
# Cerulea
Lying Right in the center of the continent East of Debrica, South of Sprout, West of Vorth. Most of the kingdom is a large island roughly oval in shape, oriented northeast to southwest. Connected to the mainland by Four Bridges Connecting to the major cerulean cities.
Cerulea is one of the smallest kingdoms but it has maintained its power with its cleaver use of terrain. It is said in cerulean waters there are as many enemy skulls as there are fishes.
Cerulea is inhabited by Dermen's or as others call them Ceruderm(Blue colored men) and various other species found in Aridonia in small groups.
