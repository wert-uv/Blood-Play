## 🧬 Traits
### Race Traits
* **Blood Fiends boon:** When unconscious if you fail a will save you loose control for N*your lvl min (for this roll you cant add any modifiers) In this time your  Res and Hp will be doubled and you will be classified as a fiend.
* **Blood Concentration:** When an ally is hurt you can choose to take the damage in his place which will give you +1 SM.

### Personal Traits
* **Night vision:** your vision isn't depleted at night (but in complete darkness it is)
* **Soulsight:** See a creature's alignment aura faintly (law/chaos, good/evil) after observing them for 5 minute.
