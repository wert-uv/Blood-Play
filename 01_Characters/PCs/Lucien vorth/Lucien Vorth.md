## 🛠️ Skills and Abilities

### Skills
*Usable once per long rest*
1. **SPELL WEAVE (given by Ancient emerald dragon):** Can conjure up a single spell using knowledge per long rest.
2. **NO COST:** Can cast a spell once per day without any mana.
3. **DRACONIC TRANSFORMATION:** Can turn into a dragon doubling all stats except health and mana, gives breath weapon for 5 turns after which fatigue is increased by 3 points.

### Abilities
*Usable Class Level times per day*
1. **PARALLEL CAST:** When activated the spell casted is duplicated without any mana.
2. **DAMAGE BURST:** Can double the damage of the current casted spell.
