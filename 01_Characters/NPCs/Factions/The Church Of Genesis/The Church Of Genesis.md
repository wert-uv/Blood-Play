# THE CHURCH OF GENESIS

### ORGANIZATION

**The High Engineer:**

- Supreme leader
- Direct communication with Aevita
- Current: High Engineer Meridian

**The Design Council:**

- 12 master engineers/priests
- Run church operations

**Priests (Engineers):**

- Trained in theology AND engineering
- Build, repair, innovate
- 10 years education required

**The Faithful:**

- Donate labor/skills
- Receive free services

### CORE TENETS

1. Progress is holy
2. Suffering is solvable
3. Technology serves life
4. Innovation is worship
5. Consent is sacred

### PRACTICES

**Weekly Innovation Service:**

- Showcase inventions
- Collective problem-solving
- Prayer through creation

**Ritual of Enhancement:**

- Free for faithful
- Blessed by Aevita's power
- Optional—never forced

**The Miracle Healings:**

- Free medical clinics at every church
- No questions, no payment
- "All who suffer deserve help"

### TEMPLES (CREATION CATHEDRALS)

**Architecture:**

- Gleaming metal and glass
- Working factories inside
- Beautiful AND functional
- Self-sufficient (solar, recycling)

**Features:**

- Medical clinics (free healing)
- Repair shops (fix anything)
- Academies (teach engineering)
- Research labs
- Community workshops
- Housing for homeless

**Main Cathedral:** Ferropolis, Kingdom of Rust

- 10,000+ capacity
- Aevita sometimes manifests here
- Pilgrimage site
