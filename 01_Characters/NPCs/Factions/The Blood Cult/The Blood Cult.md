## THE BLOOD CULT

### **Organization Structure**

#### **The Inner Circle - "The Crimson Vessels"**
- High Priests who have directly communicated with Nex
- Each bears multiple Blood Crystals
- Slowly transforming into blood abominations
- Completely fanatical; death means nothing

**Notable Members:**
- **Cardinal Sanguine** - Leader; absorbed 37 Blood Crystals
- **Mother Crimson** - Runs breeding programs for "perfect sacrifices"
- **The Twelve Collectors** - Hunt Blood Crystals across continents

---

#### **The Seekers - "The Devoted"**
- Priests, cultists, true believers
- Seek Blood Crystals to trade up in cult hierarchy
- Perform sacrifices and blood rituals
- Recruit new members

**Recruitment Methods:**
- Promise of immortality through blood magic
- Offering power to the desperate
- Kidnapping and brainwashing
- Infiltrating other religions

---

#### **The Sacrifices - "The Offered"**
- Victims marked for sacrifice
- Some are willing (brainwashed or fanatical)
- Others are captives
- Bloodlines are tracked; "pure blood" families kidnapped

**The Breeding Program:**
Mother Crimson runs horrifying eugenics project:
- Forced breeding of "magical bloodlines"
- Children raised as perfect sacrifices
- Most disturbing aspect of the cult

---

### **Religious Practices**

#### **The Blood Tithe**
Daily ritual where cultists offer drops of their own blood:
- Dripped onto Blood Crystal (if they have one)
- Consumed by higher-ranking member
- Collected in vials and stored
- Used in rituals and sold for funding

---

#### **The Exsanguination Ceremony**
Major sacrifice ritual:
1. Victim restrained on stone altar
2. Prayers to Nex (the "Crimson Litany")
3. Ritual bloodletting from all arteries simultaneously
4. Blood collected in ceremonial bowls
5. Blood infused with magic and consumed or crystallized
6. Victim's body preserved (organs used in other rituals)

**Purpose:** 
- Nourish Blood Crystals
- Gain blood magic powers
- Communicate with Nex
- "Feed" the imprisoned goddess

---

#### **The Crimson Baptism**
Initiation ritual for new cultists:
- Immersed in tub of blood (from previous sacrifices)
- Cuts made on palms (to mix blood with bath)
- Swear oaths while submerged
- Emerge "reborn in blood"
- Marked with blood sigil (won't wash off)

---

#### **The Fragment Communion**
When cult acquires a Blood Crystal:
- Entire congregation gathers
- Crystal displayed in ceremonial case
- Members take turns touching it
- Those who touch it experience Nex's presence
- Some go mad, some become more fanatical

---

### **Cult Beliefs**

**The Sacred Texts - "The Crimson Codex":**

"*In the beginning, there was blood, and in blood, there was truth.*"

"*Life is a book written in crimson. Death is merely turning the page.*"

"*To know the blood is to know creation. To spill blood is to read truth.*"

"*The Sealed One will return. Her prison weakens with every drop spilled in her name.*"

"*Blessed are the Offered, for their blood feeds divinity.*"

---

**Core Doctrine:**
1. Blood contains the secrets of creation
2. Nex is the only god who seeks true knowledge
3. Other gods are jealous of her wisdom (sealed her out of fear)
4. Sacrifices are holy acts that bring enlightenment
5. When Nex returns, she will reward the faithful with eternal understanding

---

**The Promise:**
Cult members believe that when Nex returns:
- Faithful will be transformed into "perfect beings"
- Given immortality and power
- Allowed to study alongside Nex
- Granted understanding of life's mysteries

**The Reality:**
Nex views her cultists as:
- Tools for gathering Blood Crystals
- Interesting subjects for study
- Possible sacrifices if needed
- Means to an end

She has zero loyalty to them. If freed, she would likely dissect her most faithful followers first ("Their devotion has surely created interesting blood properties!").

## **THE BLOOD CULT-2**

**Structure:**
- **High Priests:** Those who've drunk Nex's blood (directly controlled)
- **Flesh-Scribes:** Record her teachings in their own blood
- **Crystal Seekers:** Hunt for Blood Crystals
- **The Offered:** Volunteer test subjects (surprisingly many)

**Beliefs:**
- Life is sacred because it contains truth
- Death is necessary for understanding
- Pain is data
- The body is a book to be read
- Nex will return and read all books

**Practices:**
- Blood offerings (their own or others')
- Ritual scarification (cutting divine formulas into skin)
- Meditation on pain
- Study of anatomy
- Human sacrifice (to "add to the library")

**Hierarchy:**
Based on sacrifice:
- Level 1: Donated blood
- Level 2: Removed own finger
- Level 3: Removed own limb
- Level 4: Removed own organ (and survived)
- Level 5: Died and been resurrected by Nex

**Temples:**
- Look like medical facilities
- Operating theaters as sanctuaries
- Blood pools for baptism
- Walls covered in anatomical diagrams
- Always smell of copper and formaldehyde

---

### **FOLLOWERS**

**Demographics:**
- **Scholars:** Seek knowledge at any cost
- **Physicians:** Want medical breakthroughs
- **The Desperate:** Dying, seeking cure
- **The Broken:** Nothing left to lose
- **The Curious:** Want to understand life
- **The Mad:** Already twisted

**What They Offer:**
- Themselves (body for research)
- Their children (next generation of subjects)
- Others (kidnapping for Nex)
- Blood (lots of blood)
- Loyalty unto death (and after)

**What They Receive:**
- Knowledge (forbidden medical secrets)
- Power (blood magic abilities)
- Immortality (possibly - she's vague)
- Purpose (serving something greater)
- Connection (to Nex herself)

**The Twisted Respect:**
Nex actually values her followers:
- Documents their contributions
- Remembers every name
- Thanks them personally
- Keeps their memories after death
- Considers them "co-researchers"

But this doesn't mean she won't sacrifice them. Research comes first.
