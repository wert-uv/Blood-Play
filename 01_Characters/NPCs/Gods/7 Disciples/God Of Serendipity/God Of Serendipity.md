## TEMPUS - THE TIME BREAKER

**Domain:** Fate of Life (through time, probability, serendipity)
**Origin:** Vivestera (scholar obsessed with time)
**Appearance:** Human with pointed ears, blindfolded eyes, two small horns, asymmetric earrings (3 on one side, 1 on other)
**Alignment:** True Neutral

### ORIGIN
Mortal scholar studying time/causality. Saw True God phenomenon because his obsession with temporal mechanics made existence visible. First Seeker to perceive it (~500 FE). Followed through time, gained ability to see all possible futures.

### PERSONALITY
- **Observer:** Rarely intervenes directly
- **Melancholic:** Knows all outcomes (burden of knowledge)
- **Patient:** Has infinite time perspective
- **Cryptic:** Speaks in possibilities, not certainties

**The Blindfold:** Covers eyes because seeing all timelines simultaneously is overwhelming. Can remove it but prefers not to. Eyes show infinite possibilities when revealed.

### POWERS
- See all possible futures
- Limited time manipulation (can't change past)
- Exist in multiple timelines simultaneously
- Grant glimpses of futures to others
- Make "coincidences" happen (serendipity)

**Why He Doesn't Prevent Bad Futures:**
"Every choice creates branches. Preventing one bad future creates ten others. I observe. I guide. But I cannot choose for you. Free will is the variable that makes time meaningful."

### RELATIONSHIPS
- **All Seekers:** Neutral mediator
- **Sereth:** Friendly (both understand inevitability)
- **Imperion:** Stopped his timeline manipulation
- **Nex:** Prevented 47 of her worst experiments
- Shows visions but doesn't force compliance

### RELIGION - Chroniclers of Fate
- Philosophical schools, not temples
- Study probability and chance
- Keep records of all possibilities
- Teach acceptance of fate
- Small but intellectually influential
