## ORIGIN - THE TRUTH

### **BORN IN VIVESTERA (65 Years Before Fracturing)**

**The Winged Tribes:**
Mortal winged humanoids lived in Vivestera's mountains. NOT celestials—just mortals with natural wings.

**Sereth's Birth Defect:**
- Born with **right wing skeletal from birth**
- No flesh, no feathers—just exposed bone
- Could not fly properly
- Tribal elders debated killing her
- Mother fought for her: "She has two wings. One lives, one is bone. Perhaps this means something."

**Growing Up Different:**
- Could not fly with tribe
- Mocked by other children
- Became tribal death-keeper (prepared bodies, performed funerals)
- Developed obsession: "Why is part of me dead while I live?"

**The Dual Personality Emerges (Even as Mortal):**
- **Day-Sereth:** Gentle, compassionate, nurturing
- **Night-Sereth:** Cold, analytical, clinical about death
- Already divided before becoming celestial

---

### **THE FRACTURING (Year 0 FE)**

**What Happened:**
- Sereth (age 65) was tending dying elder when Fracturing began
- In mountains (high altitude, close to layer boundary)
- Reality tore; she was pulled upward
- **Fell through fracture-tear between Vivestera and Nirvana**

**Trapped in Nirvana:**
- Her body began celestial transformation
- But transformation went wrong:
  - Left side: Became perfect celestial
  - Right side: Remained mortal, began dying immediately
- Stuck half-celestial, half-mortal
- Unable to complete transformation or die

**The Stabilization:**
Eventually stabilized into permanent half-death:
- Right side preserved in death-transition (never completes dying)
- Left wing grew perfect celestial feathers
- Right wing remained skeletal (animated by death magic now)
- Two personalities solidified into distinct consciousnesses

**She became: The First Undead Celestial**

**NOT BECAUSE OF THE WING** (wing was always skeletal—birth defect)

### **THE ACTUAL REASON: Soul Erasure Experiments**

**Timeline (~1900 FE):**

**Nex's Request:**
"Let me study soul transformation. Observe closely."

Sereth agreed: Observation only, no interference.

**The Betrayal:**
Nex didn't just observe—she **intervened**:
- Used blood magic to stop transformation mid-process
- Wanted to see what happens
- The soul was **ERASED**—permanent destruction, impossible death

**The Confrontation:**
BOTH souls united in fury:
- "YOU DESTROYED THEM! They trusted me!"
- "That soul can NEVER be reborn! NEVER!"

Nex: "The data was invaluable. One soul for infinite understanding. Acceptable trade."

**From that moment: Absolute hatred**
