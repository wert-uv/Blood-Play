
## BACKSTORY - THE FIRST RESEARCHER

### **MORTAL LIFE (3,200 Years Ago)**

**Birth Name:** Nex (same name even as mortal - parents were scholars)
**Origin:** Unknown kingdom
**Early Life:**

Born to a family of physicians during a plague era:
- Parents were healers trying to cure a blood disease
- Entire family contracted it
- Young Nex watched them die over months
- She studied their decline, took notes, didn't cry
- Age 8: Last of family dies, she's alone
- Villagers horrified: she dissected their bodies to "understand"

**The Isolation:**
Ages 8-14: Lived alone in abandoned family home
- Continued parents' research
- Captured animals to study
- Eventually studied human corpses (from plague victims)
- Village tolerated her (needed someone who understood disease)
- She saved some lives (accidentally - was just testing theories)

**The Discovery:**
Age 14: Made breakthrough discovery:
- Blood carries "messages" - information written in its composition
- Diseases are these messages gone wrong
- But also: Life itself is written in blood
- "The universe's source code is biological"

She cured the plague. She also became obsessed.

**The Meeting (Age 15):**

The True God appeared to her while she was dissecting her hundredth corpse.

**The Conversation:**
> True God: "What are you seeking?"
> 
> Nex: "Truth. Life is a code. I will read it."
> 
> True God: "Why?"
> 
> Nex: "Because it's there. Because no one else will. Because it's beautiful."
> 
> True God: "Do you love life?"
> 
> Nex: "I don't know what love is. But I want to understand life so completely that maybe I'll learn."
> 
> True God: "Then seek. But know: to read the code, you may have to rewrite yourself."
> 
> Nex: "Then I will."

The True God touched her forehead.

### **THE TRANSFORMATION**

What the True God did:
- She started following True God a being of unsurmountable energy as he travelled soaking in the energy and magic of the old god becoming immortal.

**First Years as Seeker:**
- Spent centuries studying every species
- Created Blood Crystals (fragments of her power)
- Built the Nex Void (personal research Lab consisting of actual landmasses of Vivestera built in the [[Abyss]])
- Helped mortals (when it served research)
- Harmed mortals (when it served research)
- No distinction between help and harm

**The Divergence (2,500 Years Ago):**
At the meeting of all Seekers, she proposed:
"We should dissect the True God to understand Enlightenment."

This... did not go well.
- Sereth attacked her immediately
- Others were horrified
- Nex genuinely didn't understand why
- "It's the most efficient method."

The fight nearly killed Sereth (Nex was winning). Other Seekers intervened.

**The Sealing (2,400 Years Ago):**

After centuries of "research" that crossed every line:
- Used entire kingdoms as test subjects
- Created plagues to study blood messages
- Developed method to extract memories from blood
- Claimed to have found "soul code" in DNA

The other Seekers voted (6 to 1) to seal her away.

**The Sealing:**
- They shattered her into a million fragments of blood crystals and spread them across Vivestera, Nirvana and Tophet. 

**Current Status (600 years sealed):**

In the Nex Void:
- Body in stasis (unchanging, unaging)
- Consciousness active
- Continues research on stored samples
- Manifests through crystals scattered across Vivestera
- Patiently waits for her followers to gather crystals
- Goal: Accumulate enough power to return
- True Goal: Get her hands on the True God for study
