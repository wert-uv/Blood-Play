Nex proposed dissecting the True God. Sereth, who believes in sacred cycles and respecting death, was horrified. She attacked Nex immediately.

**The Fight:**

- Reshapped landscape
- Nearly killed Sereth (multiple times)

Sereth has not forgiven this.
