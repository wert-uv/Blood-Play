## DRAVITUS - THE FIRST DRAGON

**Domain:** Environment of Life (through elemental forces)
**Origin:** Tophet (trapped during Fracturing)
**Appearance:** Humanoid with shark teeth, dragon wings, snake eyes, flame hair, body shifts between elements
**Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral

### ORIGIN
Mortal caught in Tophet during Fracturing. Survived by bonding with all elements. Became first to master elemental transformation. Saw True God phenomenon moving through elemental chaos—his mastery of elements made it visible.

### PERSONALITY
- **Prideful:** Believes dragons/elements superior
- **Passionate:** Everything done with intensity
- **Destructive:** Creation through destruction (forge mentality)
- **Honorable:** Keeps promises, respects strength

### POWERS
- Master all elements (fire, water, earth, air, lightning, hybrid)
- Transform between elemental forms
- Summon primordial dragons
- Create elemental catastrophes
- Birth new elements through fusion

### RELATIONSHIPS
- **Vorgath:** Rivals but respect each other (fought 3-day draw)
- **Aevita:** Allies (combines tech with elements)
- **Nex:** Indifferent (not his problem)
- **Imperion:** Loose alliance (both from Tophet)

### RELIGION - Temples of the Forge
- Decentralized elemental worship
- Each element has own priests
- Forges as sacred sites
- Vorth Empire primary worshippers
- Dragon-riders especially devoted
