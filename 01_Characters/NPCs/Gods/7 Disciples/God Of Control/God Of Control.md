## IMPERION - GOD OF CONTROL

**Domain:** Way of Life (through order, control, perfection)
**Origin:** Tophet (brought order to elemental chaos)
**Appearance:** Slim tall elf, pink pupils with infinite nested circles (hypnotic), perfect symmetry
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral (verging Evil)

### ORIGIN
Mortal trapped in Tophet's chaos. Survived by imposing order—creating patterns in randomness. Saw True God phenomenon because his obsession with control made underlying order of existence visible. Began systematizing chaos itself.

### PERSONALITY
- **Controlling:** Everything must be ordered, optimized
- **Calculating:** Every action planned twelve steps ahead
- **Pragmatic:** End justifies means (dangerous philosophy)
- **Patient:** Willing to wait centuries for plans
- **Convinced:** Believes he's right, others wrong

**The Eyes:** Pink pupils contain infinite nested circles—looking into them shows your place in his perfect order. Hypnotic effect. Most look away quickly.

### POWERS
- Mind control (can override free will)
- Perfect prediction (through pattern recognition)
- Create zones of absolute order
- Rewrite laws of reality locally
- Compel obedience from those who accept his authority

### THE SCHISM (2847 FE)
**What Happened:**
- Concluded free will prevents enlightenment
- Began manipulating world events subtly
- Installing puppet rulers
- Creating surveillance networks
- Rewriting laws to increase control

**Why He Stopped:**
- Tempus detected anomalies
- Other Seekers united against him
- Realized he was isolated
- Withdrew to Tophet rather than fight

**Current Status:**
- Watches from isolation
- Still has followers (authoritarian regimes)
- Plotting return? Unknown
- Other Seekers monitor carefully

### RELATIONSHIPS
- **Aevita:** Sympathizes with goals, opposes methods
- **Imperion's View:** "She could be perfect if she'd stop respecting free will"
- **Nex:** Mutual respect (both pragmatic, results-oriented)
  - **Dangerous:** Might ally with Nex if she returns
  - Only Seeker who could side with her
- **Vorgath:** Mutual disdain (control vs freedom)
- **Tempus:** Adversary (Tempus preserves free will/choice)

### RELIGION - The Eternal Order
- Totalitarian hierarchical structure
- Followers in authoritarian kingdoms
- Preach perfect society through control
- Controversial/feared by free peoples
- Small but powerful

**The Danger:**
If Nex returns and Imperion sides with her (pragmatic alliance), the other Seekers face serious problem. Nex's power + Imperion's strategy = catastrophic combination.
