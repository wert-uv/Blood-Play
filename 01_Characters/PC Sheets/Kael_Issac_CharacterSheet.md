## 🧬 Traits
### Race Traits
* **Blood Fiends boon:** When unconscious if you fail a will save you loose control for N*your lvl min (for this roll you cant add any modifiers) In this time your Str and Res will be doubled and you will be classified as a fiend.
* **Blood Curse:** when you Hit a creatures CHP your strength is increased by +1 for 3 turns But your INT is lowered by 1 for 5 turns.

### Personal Traits
* **Night vision:** your vision isn't depleted at night (but in complete darkness it is)
* **Soulsight:** See a creature's alignment aura faintly (law/chaos, good/evil) after observing them for 5 minute.

## 🛠️ Skills and Abilities

### Skills
*Usable once per long rest*
1. **SILENT TAKEDOWN:** Instantly kill unaware humanoid < Player level (Res save negates).
2. **Hex:** Curse target: Take +PC Level necrotic damage per hit. Moves to new target if killed (1hr).

### Abilities
*Usable Class Level times per day*
1. **ELDRITCH BLAST:** Fire a beam of crackling energy. Wd10 +pc LVL force damage + push target 5ft. Scales with Will.
2. **PHASE SHIFT:** Immune to all physical dmg for the turn the ability is used. Can phase through solid matter.
3. **SHADOW STEP:** You can teleport from one shadow to another in a radius of 50ft.
