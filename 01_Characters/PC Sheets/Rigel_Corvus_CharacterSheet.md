## Skills and Abilities
### Skills

Skills can be used once per long rest

1. **Perfect structure**- u will drop to 1 hp for the duration of this skill (5 turns) u will gain 100 RN and +20 additional RN on attack rolls involving bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage and will restore all chp at the end of the skill and 1 additional  dice on attack roll.
2. **Furry-** all physical attacks become 5ft AOE and they deal 2X dmg for next 3 turns.

### Abilities

Ability can be used the same times as class level per day

1. **Protection Swing**- you can block an attack towards an ally if in diameter of 30ft and counter attack(take a action) in that turn ur attack will have +3 dice roll. 
2. **Improved War Magic**- when you use your action to cast a spell, you can make one weapon attack as a bonus action.
3. **Adaptive Guard-** you can take an action if the enemy attacks you in that turn ur attack will have +3 dice roll.
