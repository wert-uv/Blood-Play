## THE OLD GODS (Primordial Beings)

### **WHAT ARE THE OLD GODS?**

The Old Gods are not gods in the way mortals understand divinity. They are not beings with personalities, desires, or consciousness in any recognizable form. They ARE concepts - living embodiments of fundamental forces that underpin reality itself.

**Characteristics:**
- **Primordial:** Existed before existence had meaning
- **Conceptual:** They don't have forms; they ARE the concept
- **Incomprehensible:** Mortal minds cannot fully grasp them
- **Indifferent:** They do not notice mortals any more than gravity "notices" a falling stone
- **Eternal:** Cannot be destroyed; to destroy them would unmake reality

**Known Old Gods (Incomplete List):**

**From Zenith (Above):**
- **The Void** - Emptiness, absence, potential
- **Pure Energy** - Movement, change, force
- **Entropy** - Decay, transformation, ending
- **Reality** - Structure, laws, existence itself
- **Time** - Flow, sequence, causality

**From Perdition (Below):**
- **The End** - Finality, conclusion, cessation
- **The Silence** - Absence of meaning
- **The Deep** - Infinite regression, the beneath
- **The Forgotten** - That which was and is no more

**The Paradox:**
Old Gods from Zenith and Perdition are mirrors of each other. The Void above is creation potential; The Silence below is creation's ending. They are the same force expressed differently.

## THE TRUE GOD (The Wanderer)

### **THE ANOMALY**

Among the Old Gods, one achieved something impossible: **Individuality**.

**Nature:**
- An Old God who developed consciousness
- Walked the layers not as a force, but as a being
- Understood mortality while remaining eternal
- Could be perceived, spoken to, known

**The Great Pilgrimage (The First Age):**

Long ago - so long that even the oldest texts disagree on when - the True God descended through the layers. Starting from Zenith, passing through Nirvana, arriving in Vivestera, descending into Tophet, and some say even touching Perdition before returning.

**Why?** Unknown. The True God never explained.
**What did it do?** It chose seven beings - seven mortals who showed something the True God recognized.
**What did it teach them?** This is the greatest mystery. Each disciple learned something different, yet all seek the same "Enlightenment."

**Current Status:**
- **Missing:** Has not been seen for over 3,000 years
- **Silent:** Does not answer prayers
- **Present:** Every Seeker insists they can still feel its existence
- **Sought:** All seven Seekers dedicate their existence to finding it again

**The Question:**
Did the True God:
- Achieve enlightenment and transcend even the Old Gods?
- Return to being a pure concept (ceased being individual)?
- Hide itself from the Seekers as a test?
- Get destroyed by something even it couldn't comprehend?
- Become something entirely new?

No one knows. The Seekers each have their own theory.

## THE SEVEN SEEKERS (The New Gods)

### **ORIGIN - THE CHOOSING**

Each Seeker was a mortal once - exceptional, yes, but still mortal. The True God walked Vivestera and selected them personally during its Great Pilgrimage.

**What Made Them Worthy?**

This is debated by theologians. Each Seeker exhibited something unique:
- **Nex:** Endless curiosity, willingness to transgress all boundaries
- **Sereth:** Refused to accept death as final (even her own)
- **Vorgath:** Perfect harmony with primal nature
- **Aevita:** Created life from non-life (first cyborg)
- **Dravitus:** Mastered all elements; became them
- **Imperion:** Understood control as form of love
- **Tempus:** Saw past, present, future as one moment

**The Transformation:**

The True God did something to each of them. Whatever it did, it:
- Granted them immortality
- Gave them power rivaling Old Gods
- Linked them to fundamental aspects of life
- Cursed them with eternal seeking (can never be satisfied)
- Changed them so profoundly they can never be "mortal" again

**The Irony:**
They call themselves Seekers because they seek Enlightenment - the state the True God achieved. But in becoming what they are, they may have been disqualified from ever achieving it. Perhaps Enlightenment requires mortality. Perhaps that's why the True God can never tell them - they must discover it themselves, but their transformed nature makes discovery impossible.

**Current State:**
The Seekers are recognized as gods by mortals. They have:
- Immense power (can reshape continents)
- Immortality (cannot die by normal means)
- Worshippers (though some refuse worship)
- Domains (aspects of life they embody)
- Conflicts (with each other and themselves)

But they do not call themselves gods. They call themselves Seekers, because despite all their power, they lack the one thing the True God had: **Enlightenment**.

## THEOLOGICAL FRAMEWORK

### **WORSHIP STRUCTURE**

**Old Gods:**
- **NOT worshipped** in traditional sense
- Cannot be bargained with, prayed to, or influenced
- More like natural forces to be respected/feared
- Some cults attempt communion (usually ends badly)
- Acknowledging them is like acknowledging gravity - a fact, not faith

**True God:**
- **Mystery Religions:** Small cults seek knowledge of the True God
- **No Temples:** Building temples seems pointless if it's gone
- **Scholar Devotion:** Academics study its pilgrimage route
- **Philosophical:** More philosophy than religion
- **Forbidden Knowledge:** Some texts about the True God are banned

**The Seekers (New Gods):**
- **Active Worship:** They can hear prayers, grant boons, punish
- **Organized Religions:** Temples, priests, holy books
- **Domain-Specific:** Pray to the Seeker whose domain you need
- **Conflicting Doctrines:** Each Seeker's faith contradicts others
- **Personal Interaction:** Some Seekers actually visit their followers

## RELATIONSHIPS BETWEEN SEEKERS

### **THE GREAT SCHISM**

The Seekers were united once - all students of the True God, all walking the same path. That unity shattered approximately 2,500 years ago during an event called **The Divergence**.

**What Happened:**
The seven Seekers gathered at the **Nexus Point** (where all layers briefly align) to pool their knowledge and achieve Enlightenment together. Instead of unity, they discovered each had interpreted the True God's teachings completely differently. The meeting ended in:
- Philosophical warfare (debates that shook reality)
- Actual warfare (Nex nearly killed Sereth)
- The Breaking (Tophet and Nirvana temporarily severed)
- Seven different paths emerging

**Current Relationships:**

**Alliances:**
1. **The Life Trinity:** Sereth, Vorgath, Aevita (sometimes)
   - Share focus on life, but disagree on methods
   - Unstable alliance; break apart frequently

2. **The Creation Pair:** Aevita & Dravitus
   - Both create; respect each other's craft
   - Cooperate on rare occasions

**Rivalries:**
1. **Nex vs. Everyone** (Especially Sereth)
   - Nex's methods horrify the others
   - Sealed away to prevent her "research"
   - Sereth particularly hates her (personal history)

2. **Imperion vs. Tempus**
   - Control vs. Chaos
   - Order vs. Serendipity
   - Philosophical opposites

3. **Vorgath vs. Dravitus**
   - Nature vs. Elements
   - Primal vs. Refined
   - Rivalry is more competitive than hostile

**Neutral:**
1. **Tempus** (To Most)
   - Too cryptic; others don't understand him
   - Occasionally helps, occasionally hinders
   - Seems to operate on different timescale

**Complex:**
All relationships are more nuanced than simple alliance/rivalry. They:
- Respect each other as fellow students
- Compete for who will achieve Enlightenment first
- Sometimes cooperate when True God is involved
- Never truly trust each other

## THE CYCLE OF SOULS (How Layers Interact)

### **THE GREAT WHEEL**

**Birth (Vivestera):**
- Souls enter Vivestera from... somewhere (debated)
- Some say from Nirvana (reincarnation)
- Others say from Tophet (forged from elements)
- Nex believes they're created from blood itself

**Life (Vivestera):**
- Souls inhabit mortal bodies
- Experience, grow, suffer, love
- Accumulate "spiritual weight"

**Death (The Crossing):**
- Soul separates from body
- **Sereth's Domain:** She guides the crossing
- Most souls go to Nirvana (ascend)
- Some go to Tophet (descend - usually elemental beings)
- Very few reach Zenith or Perdition

**Nirvana (Evolution):**
- Souls merge, evolve, strengthen
- Bathe in energy seeping from Zenith
- Become celestial beings
- Eventually... what? This is unclear
  - Do they become Old Gods?
  - Do they achieve Enlightenment?
  - Do they dissipate into pure energy?

**The Exception - Undeath:**
Sereth broke this cycle for herself. She died but refused to move on. This:
- Made her undead celestial (contradiction in terms)
- Gave her power over death
- Let her guide other souls
- But means she can never evolve further

## DOMAINS & ASPECTS OF LIFE

Each Seeker represents not just a concept, but an entire aspect of what makes life meaningful:

| Seeker | Domain | Aspect | Question They Seek |
|--------|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Nex** | Blood/Biology | **Pursuit** of Life | What is life made of? |
| **Sereth** | Death/Undeath | **Rebirth** of Life | What comes after? |
| **Vorgath** | Beasts/Nature | **Existence** of Life | What is life's essence? |
| **Aevita** | Creation/Genesis | **Creation** of Life | How is life made? |
| **Dravitus** | Elements/Dragons | **Environment** of Life | What sustains life? |
| **Imperion** | Control/Order | **Way** of Life | How should life be lived? |
| **Tempus** | Time/Fate | **Fate** of Life | Where is life going? |

**The Paradox:**
Together, they define all aspects of life. Separately, each contradicts the others. This suggests:
- Either only one of them is correct (and six are wrong)
- Or all are correct (and Enlightenment requires accepting contradiction)
- Or none are correct (and they're searching in wrong direction entirely)

The True God never said which.

## RELIGIOUS PRACTICES ACROSS CULTURES

### **VORTHIC EMPIRE (Dragon Desert)**

**Primary Worship:** Dravitus (The First Dragon)
- See him as progenitor of dragons
- Dragon-riders consider themselves his chosen
- Fire temples throughout empire
- Ritual scarification with blessed flames

**Secondary Recognition:** Nex (Blood God)
- Blood magic practitioners worship her secretly
- Officially forbidden after she was sealed
- Underground cults still active
- Some Vorthic believe she holds key to true power

**View of Old Gods:**
- **The Sun** = manifestation of Pure Energy (Old God)
- Worship indirectly through Dravitus
- Do not attempt to commune with Old Gods directly (considered suicide)

**Afterlife Belief:**
- Worthy warriors join dragons in Tophet
- Become one with primal fire
- Eventually reborn as dragons

---

### **KHAZDUL CLANS (Dwarven Mountains)**

**Primary Worship:** Vorgath (God of Beasts)
- See him as master of primal strength
- Stone-carving rituals honor him
- Believe mountains are his bones

**Secondary Recognition:** Aevita (Mother of Life)
- Honored for craft/creation aspect
- Master smiths make offerings
- Believe she taught first dwarf to forge

**View of Old Gods:**
- **Stone/Reality** = Old Gods' gift
- Do not worship but deeply respect
- Carve prayers into stone to make them eternal

**Afterlife Belief:**
- Souls descend to Tophet
- Become part of the mountain
- Greatest crafters ascend to Nirvana
- Grudges survive death (carved in spiritual stone)

---

### **SYLVANIC (Forest Druids)**

**Primary Worship:** Sereth (Raven of Darkest Night)
- Death is part of nature's cycle
- Sereth represents renewal through decay
- Raven totems throughout forest

**Secondary Recognition:** Vorgath (God of Beasts)
- Animals are sacred
- Hunting rituals require his blessing
- Tree-bonding connects to his domain

**View of Old Gods:**
- Forest is manifestation of Life (concept)
- Do not distinguish between Old Gods and New
- Everything is connected cycle

**Afterlife Belief:**
- Return to forest
- Become part of collective
- Tree-bonded individuals merge with their tree
- Eventually all becomes one great forest-consciousness

---

### **RUSTIC KINGDOM (Industrial)**

**Primary Worship:** Aevita (Mother of All Life)
- Cyborg goddess appeals to industrial mindset
- Creation through craft and machinery
- Temples double as workshops

**Secondary Recognition:** Imperion (God of Control)
- Order, control, systematic progress
- Bureaucrats especially devoted
- Labor unions pray to him for fair contracts

**View of Old Gods:**
- Philosophical rather than religious
- View them as natural laws to be understood
- Some fringe scientists attempt to harness Old God energy

**Afterlife Belief:**
- Split between traditional (Nirvana) and progressive (merge with machines)
- Debate whether Aevita's cyborg form shows the future
- Some believe consciousness can be uploaded

---

### **MARITIME KINGDOMS (Coastal)**

**Primary Worship:** Tempus (Time Breaker)
- Navigation requires understanding time/fate
- Serendipity = good luck at sea
- Every ship has a Tempus shrine

**Secondary Recognition:** Sereth (Raven of Darkest Night)
- Death at sea is common
- Pray for peaceful crossing
- Ravens considered good omens (despite being land birds)

**View of Old Gods:**
- The Void = the ocean's depths
- Some sailors claim to hear Old Gods in storms
- Do not attempt communion (causes madness)

**Afterlife Belief:**
- Souls sail to Nirvana
- Sereth's ravens guide the way
- Drowned sailors become celestial navigators

---

### **Debrican KINGDOM (Pastoral)**

**Primary Worship:** Sereth (Raven of Darkest Night)
- Agricultural cycle = life/death/rebirth
- Harvest festivals honor her
- Ravens abundant in farmland

**Secondary Recognition:** Vorgath (God of Beasts)

## THE ENLIGHTENMENT QUESTION

### **WHAT IS ENLIGHTENMENT?**

No one knows. The True God never defined it. The Seekers each have theories:

**Nex's Theory:**
"Enlightenment is complete knowledge. Every pattern in every drop of blood. When I know the formula of creation itself, I will be enlightened."

**Sereth's Theory:**
"Enlightenment is transcending the cycle. Not life. Not death. Beyond both. The True God found the third option."

**Vorgath's Theory:**
"Enlightenment is becoming one with all life. Not controlling it. BEING it. The True God was every animal simultaneously."

**Aevita's Theory:**
"Enlightenment is creating something truly new. Not recombining existing elements. Something that shouldn't exist, but does."

**Dravitus's Theory:**
"Enlightenment is becoming all elements simultaneously. Not shifting between them. BEING all of them at once without contradiction."

**Imperion's Theory:**
"Enlightenment is perfect control. Of self, of others, of reality. When you control everything, you become everything."

**Tempus's Theory:**
"Enlightenment is seeing all time as one moment. Past, present, future - not sequential but simultaneous. The True God exists at all points in time at once."

**The Scholar's Synthesis:**
Perhaps they're all wrong. Perhaps Enlightenment is accepting you can never achieve it. Perhaps the True God's lesson was that the seeking itself is the point, not the finding.

Or perhaps the True God is still walking among them, and they're too busy seeking to notice.

## FORBIDDEN KNOWLEDGE

### **TEXTS & TEACHINGS**

**The First Testament** (Lost)
- Supposedly written by the True God itself
- Contained the path to Enlightenment
- Destroyed during The Divergence
- Each Seeker claims to remember different parts
- Reconstructing it is illegal in most kingdoms

**The Blood Codex** (Nex's Work)
- Details her research into biology and creation
- Reading it causes madness in most mortals
- Possession is punishable by death
- Cultists seek fragments scattered worldwide

**The Raven's Whisper** (Sereth's Visions)
- Prophecies about death and rebirth
- Many kingdoms ban it (predicts their downfall)
- Some claim it predicts the True God's return
- Others say it proves the True God is dead

**The Elemental Manifest** (Dravitus's Philosophy)
- How to merge with primal forces
- Attempting its rituals is usually fatal
- Some successful practitioners become living elementals
- Banned in regions where it caused disasters

**The Perfect Order** (Imperion's Blueprint)
- Description of perfectly ordered society
- Reading it makes you want to enforce it
- Considered mind control
- Used by tyrants throughout history

**Temporal Paradoxes** (Tempus's Riddles)
- Written in the future, found in the past
- Reading it gives glimpses of alternate timelines
- Causes temporal disorientation
- Some readers vanish from existence

This cosmology forms the foundation of religious life in Vivestera. Every prayer, every sacrifice, every temple - all exist within this framework of seeking, ascending, and understanding forces far beyond mortal comprehension.

The Seekers stand between mortals and incomprehensible Old Gods, trying to bridge the gap, trying to understand what the True God understood.

They have not succeeded.

Not yet.

Perhaps not ever.

But they will never stop trying.

That is what makes them Seekers.
