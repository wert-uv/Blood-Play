# ARIDONIA - MASTER GEOGRAPHY & BIOME ATLAS
## Comprehensive Survey of Landscape, Climate, and Ecological Zones

---

## 🌍 CONTINENTAL OVERVIEW

**Type:** Archipelagic Continent
**Position:** Southeastern Vivestera
**Geography:** East-west elongated landmass comprising one major eastern continent and extensive western island chains.
**Climate:** Ranges from Maritime Temperate (West) to Arid Continental (East).

---

## 🏔️ MAJOR GEOGRAPHIC REGIONS

### 1. The Western Archipelago
A chain of humid, forested islands dominated by maritime weather patterns.
- **Northwest Cluster:** Includes **BitterMarsh** (Wetlands) and scattered rocky islets.
- **West-Central Island:** Largest island, hosting **Kingdom of Rust** and **City of FylloDraya**. Features rolling temperate forests and coastal plains.
- **Southern Islands:** Includes **Kingdom of Debrica** (mainland connection) and **Ruins of Prauka** (eroded peninsula).

### 2. The Central Islands
- **Sprout Kingdom:** Isolated north-central island. Dense **Maritime Rainforest** with 80-90% old-growth coverage.
- **Kingdom of Cerulea:** South-central region (island/mainland hybrid). heavily forested with central highlands and deep coastal bays.

### 3. The Central Mountains (The Great Divide)
**Location:** Runs North-South through the center of the eastern landmass.
- **Features:** High alpine peaks (3000-4000m), glacial valleys, volcanic origins.
- **Climate Impact:** Creates a massive **Rain Shadow** effect, blocking moisture from the east.
- **Settlements:** **Kingdom of Ayaman** (Western slopes).

### 4. The Eastern Continent
- **Northern Westallis:** **Boreal/Taiga Forests**. Cold, coniferous-dominated region with fjord-like inlets.
- **Vorth Empire (The East):** Dominated by the **SasSert Desert** (Rain Shadow Desert).
    - **Vorth Loge:** Highland fortress plateau.
    - **SasSert Desert:** Rocky badlands, sand dunes (Ergs), and salt flats.
    - **Vorth Harbour:** Coastal desert (possible cold current influence).

## 🌿 BIOME ZONES & ECOLOGY

### Zone 1: Western Maritime Rainforest
- **Location:** Sprout Kingdom, Western Cerulea, BitterMarsh.
- **Climate:** Cool summers, mild winters, high rainfall (2000-3500mm).
- **Vegetation:** Massive old-growth trees, epiphytes, ferns.

### Zone 2: Temperate Deciduous Forest
- **Location:** Kingdom of Debrica, Kingdom of Rust, Eastern Cerulea.
- **Climate:** Distinct seasons, moderate rainfall (800-1400mm).
- **Vegetation:** Oak, Beech, Maple. Rich agricultural soils.

### Zone 3: Montane Forest & Alpine Tundra
- **Location:** Central Mountain Range (Ayaman).
- **Elevation:**
    - *Lower:* Mixed forest.
    - *Upper:* Conifers.
    - *Alpine (2800m+):* Tundra, permanent snow, lichens.

### Zone 4: Boreal Forest (Taiga)
- **Location:** Northern Westallis.
- **Climate:** Long, cold winters. Short growing season.
- **Vegetation:** Spruce, Fir, Pine.

### Zone 5: Rain Shadow / Scrubland
- **Location:** Eastern foothills of Central Mountains.
- **Climate:** Semi-arid (250-500mm rain).
- **Vegetation:** Juniper, Sagebrush, drought-resistant shrubs.

### Zone 6: Cold Desert
- **Location:** Interior Vorth Empire (SasSert Desert).
- **Climate:** Extreme temperature variation, very low rainfall (<150mm).
- **Features:** Rocky Hamada, Sand Dunes, Oases (Groundwater fed).

---

## 💧 HYDROLOGY

- **Rivers:** Major systems flow West from the Central Mountains through Debrica and Cerulea. Eastern rivers are rare/seasonal (Wadis).
- **Lakes:** Glacial lakes in Westallis; Debrica Lake Chain.
- **Oceans:**
    - *Western Ocean:* Stormy, brings moisture.
    - *Central Island Sea:* Protected, major trade routes.
    - *Southern Ocean:* Deep, potential cold currents.

---

## ⚔️ STRATEGIC GEOGRAPHY

1.  **Mountain Passes:** 3 major passes control East-West trade.
2.  **Vorth Harbour:** The only deep-water port for the Eastern Desert.
3.  **Central Strait:** Naval choke point between Western Mainland and Sprout Kingdom.
4.  **Fort Visselet & Fort Kettinis:** Guard maritime approaches.
