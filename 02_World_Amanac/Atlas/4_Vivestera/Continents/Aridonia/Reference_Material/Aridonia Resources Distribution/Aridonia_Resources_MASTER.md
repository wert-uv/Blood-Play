# ARIDONIA - MASTER RESOURCE COMPENDIUM
## Complete Survey of Natural Resources, Minerals, and Strategic Materials

---

## 🌍 OVERVIEW

Aridonia's resource distribution reflects its dramatic geological history. The central mountain range, formed by ancient tectonic collision, hosts rich mineral deposits. Western maritime regions provide timber and agricultural wealth, while eastern deserts contain unique minerals and fossil resources. The continent's diverse geology creates regional specialization and drives inter-regional trade.

**Resource Provinces:**
1. **Western Forests:** Timber, Agriculture (Westallis, Debrica)
2. **Central Mountains:** Metals, Gems, Stone (Ayaman, Rust)
3. **Eastern Desert:** Salt, Rare minerals, Fossils (Vorth Empire)
4. **Coastal Zones:** Fish, Salt, Pearls (Cerulea, Islands)
5. **Underground:** Deep minerals, Geothermal

---

## ⚒️ METALLIC RESOURCES

### IRON & STEEL
*Critical for military and industrial power.*

#### **Ayarson Magnetite (Kingdom of Ayaman)**
- **Type:** Highest quality magnetite; 65-70% iron content.
- **Location:** Deep mines in Ayarson Mountains (1,200m+).
- **Production:** 300,000 tons/year.
- **Strategic Value:** Basis of Dwarven military power and "Ayarson Steel".

#### **Rust Iron (Kingdom of Rust)**
- **Type:** Hematite (reddish ore) and ancient iron sands.
- **Location:** Kingdom of Rust foothills and northern beaches.
- **History:** Kingdom named for the oxidized red soil/ore.
- **Special Resource:** **Ironwood Trees** (biological iron accumulation in wood).

### COPPER
*Essential for bronze, coinage, and magic.*

#### **Mountain Copper**
- **Location:** Central mountains (Ayaman, Rust).
- **Type:** Chalcopyrite; 1-3% copper.

#### **Cerulean Copper (Kingdom of Cerulea)**
- **Location:** Fort Visselet region and Cerulea.
- **Type:** Native copper and Malachite.
- **Quality:** High; often found with magical traces ("Green Copper").
- **Uses:** Bronze, roofing for wealthy, coinage.

### TIN
*Rare and strategic.*
- **Location:** Northern mountain streams and Ruins of Prauka (Prauka Tin Streams).
- **Status:** Scarcity increasing; critical for bronze production.

### PRECIOUS METALS

#### **Gold**
- **River Gold:** Found in Debrica rivers ("Gold-Wash Creek") and Vorth Loge fields.
- **Vein Gold:** Deep mines in Ayarson ("Gilded-Reach").
- **Desert Gold:** Surface nuggets in SasSert Desert.
- **Value:** Standard currency base (1 GP = 50 SP).

#### **Silver**
- **Mountain Silver:** Ayarson and Vorth Loge area.
- **Desert Silver:** Native silver in Vorth Empire volcanic rocks.
- **Special Types:** 
    - **Moonsilver:** Glows faintly, found during full moons.
    - **Spell Silver:** Conductive to magic.
- **Value:** 1 GP = 10 SP.

#### **Platinum**
- **Location:** Extremely rare deep mines in Ayarson.
- **Uses:** Royal regalia, divine artifacts.

## 💎 GEMSTONES & MAGICAL CRYSTALS

### PHYSICAL GEMS
- **Diamonds:** Kimberlite Crater (Central Mountains).
- **Sapphires:** **Cerulea** (famous deep blue stones).
- **Emeralds:** **Sprout Kingdom** (remote canyons) and Northern Westallis.
- **Rubies:** **Vorth Empire** ("Dragon's Blood Crystals").
- **Amethyst:** **Ruins of Prauka** and Ayaman geodes.
- **Opals:** Ancient volcanic regions.

### MAGICAL STONES
- **Manastones:** Clear crystals near ley lines; store magical energy.
- **Soulstones:** Deep purple; found in ancient battlefields (Necromancy).
- **Lifestones:** Green crystals from **Sprout Kingdom** old-growth forests (Healing).
- **Dragonshards:** Crystallized dragon scales (Vorth Empire); amplify fire magic.
- **Ether Crystals:** High peaks (Ayaman); allow ethereal travel.

## 🧱 BUILDING & INDUSTRIAL MATERIALS

### STONE
- **Granite:** Ubiquitous in mountains. **Ayarson Granite** is famous for durability.
- **Marble:** **Ayaman** (White-Mountain Quarry) and **BitterMarsh** (Bitter Marble).
- **Sandstone:** Primary building material in **Vorth Empire**.
- **Slate:** **Kingdom of Rust** (roofing).
- **Obsidian:** Volcanic regions; used for blades and magic.

### CLAY & CERAMICS
- **Pottery Clay:** **Kingdom of Debrica** (Debrica Clay Beds) - famous red/gray ware.
- **Porcelain Clay (Kaolin):** **Kingdom of Westallis** - fine white ceramics.
- **Fireclay:** Kingdom of Rust (for furnaces).

### GLASS
- **Silica Sand:** Southern coasts and Vorth desert.
- **Desert Glass:** **Glass Sea (Vorth Empire)** - natural glass from lightning/impacts.

---

## 🔥 FUEL & ENERGY

- **Coal:** **Kingdom of Rust** (massive deposits, anthracite) and **Westallis**.
- **Peat:** **BitterMarsh** wetlands (fuel for locals).
- **Natural Gas/Oil:** Seeps in **Vorth Empire** (used for waterproofing/rituals).
- **Timber/Charcoal:** **Westallis** and **Debrica** forests.
- **Mithril:** **Kingdom of Ayaman** (Deepest mines); lighter/stronger than steel.
- **Adamantine:** Meteorite fragments (Extremely rare).

---

## 🧂 SALTS & CHEMICALS

- **Rock Salt:** **Vorth Empire** (Salt Flats - "White Gold").
- **Sea Salt:** Coastal regions (Cerulea).
- **Sulfur:** Volcanic vents (Ayaman).
- **Mercury (Cinnabar):** Rare volcanic deposits.

---

## 🌾 AGRICULTURAL WEALTH

- **Fertile Valleys:** **Debrica** and **Westallis** (Grains, vegetables).
- **Orchards:** **Cerulea** (Coastal fruits).
- **Dates/Oases:** **Vorth Empire**.
- **Marine Resources:** **Cerulea** and Islands (Fish, Shellfish, Pearls, Amber).

## ⚔️ RESOURCE CONFLICTS & ECONOMY

### Resource-Based Economies
- **Western Archipelago:** Timber, Fish, Ships.
- **Vorth Empire:** Salt, Dates, Silver, Glass.
- **Mountain Kingdoms (Ayaman/Rust):** Metals, Gems, Stone, Coal.

### Major Conflicts
- **Rust-Ayarson Iron War:** Fought over iron deposits 200 years ago.
- **Salt Route Raids:** Desert tribes vs Empire caravans.
- **Timber Wars:** Western kingdom border disputes.

### Future Potential
- **Deep Oil:** Undiscovered reserves in the desert.
- **Rare Earths:** Unknown minerals in deep mines.
