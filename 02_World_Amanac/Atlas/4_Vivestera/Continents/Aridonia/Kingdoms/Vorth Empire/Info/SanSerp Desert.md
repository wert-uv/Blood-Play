# EASTERN DESERT CORE - SANSERP DESERT

**Position:** East-central portion of the continent, heart of Vorth Empire
**Terrain Type:** True arid desert with multiple sub-environments
**Description:** The driest region of Aridonia, receiving less than 100mm annual precipitation. The desert name "SanSerp" appears within the Vorth Empire territory. This is a diverse desert environment including sand dunes, rocky desert, and gravel plains.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- Large dune fields in the northeastern section (erg desert)
- Rocky plateaus and mesas in the central area (hamada desert)
- A large oasis complex roughly in the geographic center
- Several smaller oases along underground water channels
- Ancient dried river channels visible from past wetter climate
- Areas of desert pavement (reg desert) in the south
- Scattered volcanic plugs or erosional remnants
- Evidence of wind erosion creating yardangs
- Salt pans in lowest elevations
- Limited vegetation except around water sources
- A few nomadic encampment areas
