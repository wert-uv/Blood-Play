# 🗺️ Vorth Empire - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** Eastern Continent (The Great East).
- **Position:** East of the Central Mountain Range.
- **Terrain:** Vast desert basins, plateaus, and badlands.

## 🌿 Biomes: Desert & Scrubland
1.  **Rain Shadow Scrub (Western Margin):** Semi-arid shrubland, juniper/sagebrush.
2.  **Cold/Cool Desert (SasSert Interior):** Rocky Hamada and Gravel Regs. Extreme temp variations.
3.  **Sand Desert (Erg):** Dune fields in the northeast and deep south.
4.  **Coastal Desert (South):** Fog-dependent coastal desert (similar to Namib/Atacama).

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **SasSert Desert:** The dominant feature. A result of the mountain rain shadow.
- **Oases:** Critical habitation zones fed by deep aquifers (e.g., Imperial Oasis).
- **Dry Wadis:** Seasonal riverbeds that flash-flood during rare storms.
- **The Glass Sea:** Natural glass flats (impact or lightning origin).
- **Salt Flats:** Dried ancient lake beds (source of "White Gold").
- **Vorth Harbour:** A deep-water port in an arid landscape, likely sustained by coastal fog harvesting and wells.
