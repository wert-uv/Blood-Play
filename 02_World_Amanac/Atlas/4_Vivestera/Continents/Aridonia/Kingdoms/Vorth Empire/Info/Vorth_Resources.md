# 🏜️ Vorth Empire - Natural Resources

## 🧂 The Salt Wealth
- **White Flats:** Massive salt flats providing unlimited **Rock Salt** (Halite).
- **"White Gold":** Salt is the empire's primary export, vital for food preservation across the continent.

## ⛏️ Mineral Wealth

### **Silver**
- **Desert Silver:** Native silver found in volcanic rocks.
- **Currency:** The Empire operates on a Silver Standard.

### **Rubies**
- **Dragon's Blood Crystals:** Deep red rubies found in marble deposits.
- **Significance:** Prized by dragon-riders; believed to strengthen bonds with dragons.

### **Glass**
- **The Glass Sea:** Natural glass formed by ancient impacts/lightning.
- **Sandstone:** Primary building material (Red, Yellow, White).

### **Dragonshards**
- **Origin:** Crystalized dragon scales/remains.
- **Use:** Powerful magical focuses for fire/elemental magic.
- **Collection:** Highly regulated; harvesting from living dragons is a capital offense.

## 🏜️ Desert Resources

### **Oases & Oil**
- **Dates:** Major food export grown in oasis networks.
- **Oil/Naptha:** Surface seeps used for waterproofing and fire rituals.
- **Nitrates:** Saltpeter from dry caves (Gunpowder potential).

---
**Economic Focus:** Salt Trade, Silver, Glass, Exotic Goods, Dates.
