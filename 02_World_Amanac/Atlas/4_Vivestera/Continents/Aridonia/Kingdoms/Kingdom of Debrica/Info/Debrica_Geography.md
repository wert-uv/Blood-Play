# 🗺️ Kingdom of Debrica (Debrica) - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** Southern Interior of Western Mainland.
- **Position:** South of Ayarson mountains.
- **Terrain:** Rolling forested hills (50-100m elevation changes) and broad river valleys.

## 🌿 Biome: Temperate Deciduous Forest
- **Climate:** Warm Temperate (Warmer than Rust).
- **Vegetation:** Dense forest (80% coverage) of broadleaf trees.
- **Seasonal:** Distinct seasons with colorful autumns.

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **River Systems:** At least three major streams flow south to the coast.
- **Lake Chain:** A system of 3 interconnected lakes in the south.
- **The "Breadbasket":** Fertile alluvial soils in river valleys make it a prime agricultural region.
- **Coastline:** Southern coast features natural bays and good harbors.
