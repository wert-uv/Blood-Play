# 🏺 Kingdom of Debrica (Debrica) - Natural Resources

## 🌾 Agricultural & Land Wealth

### **Fertile River Valleys**
- **Soil:** Deep Alluvial soil refreshed by annual floods.
- **Production:** The "Breadbasket" region; primary producer of grains and vegetables.
- **Clay Beds:** High-quality pottery clay found along riverbanks.

### **Forest Resources**
- **Timber:** Sustainable hardwoods (Oak, Beech) for construction and charcoal.
- **Charcoal:** Major producer for export to metal-poor regions.

## ⛏️ Mineral Wealth

### **River Gold**
- **Gold-Wash Creek:** Famous for placer gold deposits.
- **History:** Site of a major gold rush 50 years ago.
- **Current Status:** Steady small-scale panning production.

### **Ceramics Industry**
- **Debrica Ware:** Distinctive red and gray pottery.
- **Bricks:** Major exporter of construction bricks and tiles.

---
**Economic Focus:** Agriculture, Pottery, Timber, Gold Panning.
