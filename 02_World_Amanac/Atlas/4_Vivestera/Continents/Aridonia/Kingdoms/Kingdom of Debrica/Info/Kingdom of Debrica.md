# Kingdom of Debrica
**Position:** Southern-central portion of the island
**Terrain Type:** Rolling forested hills with river valleys
**Description:** A heavily forested kingdom occupying the southern interior. The terrain consists of gentle hills (50-100 meter elevation changes) covered in dense temperate deciduous forest. Multiple stream systems drain southward to the coast. The capital settlement is positioned on a river confluence in the approximate geographic center of the kingdom, providing access to multiple transportation routes.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- At least three major stream systems flow through the territory
- The capital is surrounded by cleared agricultural land (5 kilometers radius)
- Secondary settlements visible at river crossings (3-4 towns)
- Forest coverage approximately 80% of total territory
- A large lake or wide river section exists northeast of the capital
- Road network connects to Fort Kettinis (southwest) and Cerulea (east)
- Higher elevation ridge line forms the northern border
