# 🗺️ Kingdom of Cerulea - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** South-Central (Island/Mainland hybrid geography).
- **Position:** South of Sprout Kingdom.
- **Terrain:** Central highlands spine with coastal lowlands.

## 🌿 Biome: Maritime Temperate to Subtropical Transition
- **Climate:** Mild, wet winters and warm summers. High humidity.
- **Vegetation:** Mixed evergreen and broadleaf forests. Very dense (90% coverage on island sections).
- **Transition:** Southern areas show signs of subtropical flora (palms, ferns).

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **Central Spine:** Highland ridge running Northeast-Southwest (300-500m).
- **Coastline:** Deep bays on eastern coast; steep cliffs on western coast.
- **Hydrology:** Central river valley (~200km) is a major corridor.
- **Lakes:** Large southern lake (~25 sq km).
