# 🌊 Kingdom of Cerulea - Natural Resources

## 💎 Mineral Wealth

### **Sapphires (The Blue Wealth)**
- **Cerulea Sapphires:** Deep blue gemstones found in highland gravels.
- **Significance:** The kingdom's namesake and heraldic symbol.
- **Royal Crown:** Contains a legendary 200-carat sapphire.
- **Exports:** High-value trade to Vorth and Ayaman.

### **Copper**
- **Azure-Veins:** Native copper and malachite deposits.
- **Magic:** "Green Copper" containing magical traces found here.

## 🌊 Coastal & Marine Resources

### **Fisheries**
- **Salmon Runs:** Major seasonal food source.
- **Shellfish:** Oysters, mussels, and rare pearls.

### **Salt**
- **Sea Salt:** Evaporation ponds along the southern coast.
- **Amber:** Washes up on shores after storms.

## 🌾 Agriculture
- **Coastal Plains:** Fertile land for orchards and climate-sensitive crops.

---
**Economic Focus:** Gemstones, Copper, Marine Trade, Shipbuilding.
