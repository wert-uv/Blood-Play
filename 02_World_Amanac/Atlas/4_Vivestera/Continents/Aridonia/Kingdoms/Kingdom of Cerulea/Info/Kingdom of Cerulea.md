# KINGDOM OF CERULEA

**Position:** South of Sprout Kingdom, west of the eastern continental mainland
**Terrain Type:** Densely forested island with central highlands
**Description:** A large island roughly oval in shape, oriented northeast to southwest. The interior features modest highlands (300-500 meters elevation) creating a central spine running the length of the island. These highlands are completely forested with what appears to be mixed temperate forest transitating to coniferous at higher elevations. The capital is positioned on the eastern coast at the mouth of a river that drains the central highlands.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- Central highland ridge runs 50-60 kilometers northeast to southwest
- Multiple stream systems radiate from the central highlands
- The eastern coast has several deep bays providing natural harbors
- Western coast is more exposed with steeper cliffs
- At least 5-6 secondary settlements scattered around the coast
- A road system connects coastal settlements, following the coastline
- Agricultural land is limited to narrow coastal strips
- Forest coverage exceeds 90% of total land area
- A significant bay on the southeastern side hosts the main harbor
- Evidence of terraced farming on some coastal slopes
