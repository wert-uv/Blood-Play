# 🗺️ BitterMarsh Region - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** Extreme Northwest Island.
- **Terrain:** Low-lying wetlands, river deltas, and tidal marshes.

## 🌿 Biome: Freshwater & Coastal Wetlands
- **Vegetation:** Reeds, sedges, salt-tolerant rushes, mangroves (if warm enough) or willow/alder thickets.
- **Hydrology:** Waterlogged soils, tidal influence on coasts.

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **Crescent Shape:** The main island curves northwest to southeast.
- **Satellite Islets:** Small uninhabited marshy islands to the north.
- **Bio-Productivity:** Extremely high; nursery for fish and waterfowl.
- **Peatlands:** Interior areas contain deep peat deposits (fuel source).
