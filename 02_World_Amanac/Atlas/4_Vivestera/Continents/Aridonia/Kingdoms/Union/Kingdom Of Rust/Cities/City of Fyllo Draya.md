# City of FylloDraya
**Position:** East-central area of the island, northeast of Ayarson
**Terrain Type:** Forested hills with river valley
**Description:** Built along a major river valley that cuts through the island from the mountainous interior to the eastern coast. The city occupies both banks of this river, with denser construction on the eastern bank. Surrounding terrain consists of moderately steep forested hills (30-40 degree slopes) that create a natural defensive bowl around the settlement. The river appears navigable, with ships visible in the harbor area on the eastern coast.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- The river is approximately 50-100 meters wide at the city
- A stone bridge or series of bridges connect the two halves of the city
- Harbor facilities on the eastern coast with protected anchorage
- Terraced hillsides south of the city suggesting agriculture or vineyards
- A tributary stream joins the main river from the north
