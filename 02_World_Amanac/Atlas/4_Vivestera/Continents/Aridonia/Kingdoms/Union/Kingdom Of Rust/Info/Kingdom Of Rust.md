# Kingdom of Rust
**Position:** Northern third of the west-central island
**Terrain Type:** Mixed temperate forest and coastal plains
**Description:** Occupies the northern coastal region, characterized by gently rolling forested hills transitioning to coastal lowlands. The northern coastline is relatively smooth with a few shallow bays. Dense deciduous and mixed forests cover approximately 70% of the territory, with cleared agricultural land radiating outward from the urban center. The capital settlement is positioned inland, roughly 5-7 kilometers from the northern coast, on a slight elevation that provides defensive advantage and fresh water access.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- A river system flows from the central highlands southward, passing east of the capital
- Several small fishing villages dot the northern coast (3-4 visible settlements)
- A road network connects the capital to coastal settlements
- Forest density increases toward the interior (south)
