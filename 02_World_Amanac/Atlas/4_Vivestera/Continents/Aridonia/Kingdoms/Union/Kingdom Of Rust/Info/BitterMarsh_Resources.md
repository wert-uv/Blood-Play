# 🌫️ BitterMarsh - Natural Resources

## ⛏️ Mineral Wealth

### **Bitter Marble**
- **Appearance:** White, gray, or green-veined marble.
- **Use:** Sculpture and temple building.
- **Quarries:** Ancient quarries, some still active.

### **Bog Iron**
- **Quality:** Low-grade iron (30-40%).
- **Use:** Local tools and weapons; not exported.

## 🌫️ Wetland Resources

### **Peat**
- **Abundance:** Massive deposits in the wetlands.
- **Use:** Primary fuel source for locals; soil amendment.
- **Smell:** Distinctive smoky aroma when burned.

### **Flora**
- **Reeds & Rushes:** Used for weaving, thatching, and papermaking.
- **Medicinal Mosses:** Unique species adapted to the swamp (e.g., Bloodroot, Silverthread Moss).

---
**Economic Focus:** Subsistence, Peat, Local Crafting.
