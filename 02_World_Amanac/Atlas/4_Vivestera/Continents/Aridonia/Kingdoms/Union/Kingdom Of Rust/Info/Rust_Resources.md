# ⚒️ Kingdom of Rust - Natural Resources

## ⛏️ Mineral Wealth

### **Iron (Strategic Resource)**
- **Rust Hematite:** The kingdom is named for its reddish, iron-rich soil and ores.
- **Rust-Ford:** Open-pit mining operations.
- **Ferropolis:** Major smelting center.
- **Production:** ~150,000 tons/year (30% of continent's total).

### **Coal (Major Energy Source)**
- **Westallis/Rust Coal Fields:** Massive sedimentary basins.
- **Coal-Town:** Largest coal producer (Population 15,000).
- **Types:** Anthracite (high quality) to Bituminous.
- **Uses:** Smelting, heating, industrial power.
- **Impact:** Intense industrial smog in mining towns ("Smoke-Stack").

### **Slate**
- **Quality:** Premium roofing material.
- **quarries:** Abundant metamorphosed shale in foothills.
- **Export:** Major trade commodity for roofing tiles.

### **Rust Agate**
- **Appearance:** Banded red, orange, and rust-colored stones.
- **Uses:** Decorative polishing, building panels.

## 🌲 Flora Resources

### **Ironwood (Ferrum Lignum)**
- **Unique Resource:** Trees that bio-accumulate iron from the soil.
- **Properties:** Wood is nearly as hard as metal; iron content 5-15%.
- **Status:** **CRITICALLY RARE** due to overharvesting. Protected by law.
- **Uses:** Historical weapons/armor; now decorative curiosities.

---
**Economic Focus:** Heavy Industry, Mining, Smelting.
