# 🗺️ Kingdom of Rust - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** Western Archipelago (West-Central Major Island).
- **Position:** Northern third of the island.
- **Terrain:** Gently rolling forested hills transitioning to coastal lowlands.

## 🌿 Biome: Temperate Deciduous Forest
- **Climate:** Maritime Temperate.
- **Vegetation:** Dense deciduous forests (Oak, Beech) covering ~70% of the territory.
- **Soils:** Deep, organic-rich soils suitable for agriculture (cleared lands).

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **Central Highlands:** Southern border features rising elevation.
- **Northern Coast:** Relatively smooth with shallow bays and fishing villages.
- **Rivers:** A system flows from central highlands south, passing east of the capital.
- **Iron Accumulation:** Unique local geology leads to iron-rich soils (reddish color) and "Ironwood" trees.
