# Fort Visselet
**Position:** South-southeast of Ayarson, on southern slopes of mountain range
**Terrain Type:** Highland plateau transitioning to foothills
**Description:** A military fortification positioned at a strategic chokepoint where the mountains transition to lowlands. The fort sits at approximately 800-1000 meters elevation on a plateau that overlooks southern approaches. The terrain here is characterized by sparse forest coverage with exposed rocky outcrops, suggesting poor soil and harsh conditions. The position commands views of multiple approach routes and controls access to the mountain passes.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- A narrow valley runs south from the fort toward the coast
- Rocky badlands visible to the southwest (transition to Ruins of Prauka)
- A road/trail connects northward to Ayarson through the pass
- Small garrison settlement surrounds the main fortress
- Sparse pine and juniper forest in sheltered valleys
