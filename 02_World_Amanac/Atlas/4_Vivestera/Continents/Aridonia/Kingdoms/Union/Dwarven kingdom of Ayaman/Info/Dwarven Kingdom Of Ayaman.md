# Dwarven City of Ayarson
**Position:** West-central, at the base of significant mountain range
**Terrain Type:** Mountainous/highland with alpine forests
**Description:** Positioned at the feet of the island's primary mountain range, which runs roughly north-south through the western portion. These mountains are depicted with sharp, angular peaks suggesting recent (geologically) volcanic or tectonic formation, with elevations likely 2000-3000 meters based on snow-free peaks. The city itself is built into the mountainside and valley floor, with extensive underground development suggested by the dwarven designation. The mountains create a rain shadow effect, with lusher forests on the western (windward) slopes and drier conditions to the east.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- Mountain range extends 40-50 kilometers north-south
- At least 7-8 major peaks visible, with the tallest in the northern section
- A high mountain pass exists south of Ayarson, connecting to Fort Visselet
- Alpine meadows and coniferous forests above 1500 meters
- Evidence of mining operations (darker areas on mountainsides)
- A mountain stream descends eastward toward FylloDraya
