# 🗺️ Kingdom of Ayaman - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** Western Mainland / Central Mountain Range.
- **Position:** Built into the western slopes of the Great Divide mountains.
- **Terrain:** Steep mountainous terrain, deep valleys, and alpine peaks.

## 🌿 Biomes: Montane & Alpine
1.  **Lower Montane (600-1200m):** Mixed coniferous-deciduous forest on western slopes. High rainfall from orographic lift.
2.  **Upper Montane (1200-2000m):** Coniferous forests (Spruce, Fir).
3.  **Alpine (2000m+):** Krummholz, tundra, and bare rock/snow.

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **The Peaks:** Sharp, angular peaks (3000m+) suggesting tectonic origin.
- **City Location:** Carved into the mountainside and valley floor; extensive underground development.
- **Rain Shadow Start:** The peaks block moisture, creating the arid conditions for the East.
- **Hydrology:** Source of major river systems flowing west.
