# 🏔️ Kingdom of Ayaman (Ayarson) - Natural Resources

## ⛏️ Mineral Wealth

### **Iron & Steel (The Backbone)**
- **Ayarson Magnetite:** Massive bodies of high-grade (65-70%) iron ore.
- **Iron-Delve:** Deepest mine in the continent (1,200m).
- **Production:** 300,000 tons/year (60% of continent's total).
- **Ayarson Steel:** Renowned globally for its quality and temper.

### **Precious Metals**
- **Gold:** "Gilded-Reach" mine is the richest lode on the continent.
- **Silver:** "Moonshaft Mine" produces 30% of Aridonia's silver.
- **Platinum:** Extremely rare deep veins (Royal use only).
- **Mithril (True-Silver):** Legendary; only found in the deepest Ayarson mines (Mithril-Deep). Lighter than steel, stronger, and magic-conductive.

### **Gemstones**
- **Diamonds:** **Kimberlite Crater** - The only major diamond source.
- **Emeralds:** "Ayarson Emeralds" from metamorphic schists.
- **Granite:** **Ayarson Black Granite** - Prized for monumental architecture.
- **Marble:** "White-Mountain Quarry" (Carrara-grade).

## 🔮 Magical Resources

### **Cold Iron**
- **Properties:** Iron that disrupts Fey magic; worked without heat.
- **Uses:** Anti-magic weaponry.

### **Ether Crystals**
- **Location:** High peaks (>4,000m).
- **Properties:** Allow ethereal travel; extremely dangerous to mine.

---
**Economic Focus:** Metallurgy, Gem Cutting, Stone Quarrying, Arms Manufacturing.
