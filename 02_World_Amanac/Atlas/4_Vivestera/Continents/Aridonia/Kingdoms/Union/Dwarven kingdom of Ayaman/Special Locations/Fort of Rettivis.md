# Fort Rettivis
**Position:** Southern coastal region, southeast of Prauka, southwest of Debrica
**Terrain Type:** Coastal plains with mixed forest
**Description:** A coastal fortification positioned to guard the southern maritime approaches to the island. Built on a slight coastal elevation (20-30 meters above sea level) overlooking a natural harbor. The surrounding terrain is relatively flat coastal plain with moderate forest coverage of oak, beech, and pine. Better maintained and more active than Prauka, suggesting this is a functioning military installation.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- Protected harbor with wooden pier facilities
- Small fishing village adjacent to the fort
- Cleared fields for 2-3 kilometers inland (provisions for garrison)
- A beacon tower on the highest point
- Beach landing zones visible on both east and west sides of the promontory
- Road connecting northeast to Kingdom of Debrica
