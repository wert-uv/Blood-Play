# Ruins of Prauka
**Position:** Southwestern extension of the island
**Terrain Type:** Coastal badlands and degraded former agricultural land
**Description:** A windswept peninsula characterized by eroded terrain and abandonment. The "ruins" designation suggests this was once a thriving region, now fallen into decay. The terrain shows signs of past agricultural terracing now overgrown, collapsed stone walls, and a former harbor now silted. The coastline is extremely irregular with numerous small coves and rocky headlands. Vegetation is sparse – primarily scrub brush and wild grasses – suggesting either overgrazing, climate shift, or magical/war-related devastation.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- Remains of a circular or star-shaped fortress on a coastal promontory
- At least 15-20 ruined structures scattered across the peninsula
- Former agricultural terraces visible on south-facing slopes
- A dried or seasonal stream bed cuts through the center
- Several sea caves along the western coast
- Small rocky islands offshore to the west and south
