# 🗺️ Sprout Kingdom - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** North-Central Isolated Island.
- **Position:** In the Central Island Sea.
- **Terrain:** Mountainous island with volcanic origin (circular shape).

## 🌿 Biome: Maritime Rainforest
- **Climate:** Cool, consistently wet, oceanic.
- **Vegetation:** **Pristine Old-Growth Rainforest**. 90% coverage. Massive trees, moss, epiphytes.
- **Biology:** "Sprouting" shape of the island gives it its name.

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **Isolation:** Separated by 60-80km straits from mainland.
- **Steep Coasts:** Few natural harbors; northern coast is cliff-bound.
- **Central Peak:** Single central massif (800-1200m).
- **Waterfalls:** Numerous cascades visible from the sea.
