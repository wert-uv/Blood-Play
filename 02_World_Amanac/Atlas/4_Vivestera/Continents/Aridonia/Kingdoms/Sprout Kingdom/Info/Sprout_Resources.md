# 🍃 Sprout Kingdom - Natural Resources

## 🌿 Botanical Wealth

### **Magical Plants**
- **Lifestones:** Green crystals formed from concentrated life force in old-growth forests. Used for healing.
- **Liquid Moonlight:** Luminescent sap collected on specific nights.
- **Medicinal Herbs:** The world's most diverse collection of healing plants.

## 💎 Mineral Wealth

### **Emeralds**
- **Source:** Hydrothermal veins in deep forest canyons.
- **Legal Status:** Mining is strictly controlled/forbidden by Druidic law to protect the land.
- **Value:** Extremely high due to rarity and clarity.

## 🌲 Forest Resources
- **Ancient Timber:** Massive trees, but logging is prohibited.
- **Fauna:** Rare magical beasts and biodiversity.

---
**Economic Focus:** Natural Magic, Healing Components, Rare Herbs (Closed Economy).
