# SPROUT KINGDOM

**Position:** North-central in the archipelago, between the western major island and the eastern continent
**Terrain Type:** Heavily forested island with limited cleared land
**Description:** A medium-sized island (approximately 40-60 kilometers long, north-south orientation) almost entirely covered in dense forest. The island has an unusual shape – broader in the north with a narrower peninsula extending southward, resembling a sprouting plant (possibly the origin of the name). The terrain appears relatively flat to gently rolling, with no major mountain systems visible.

**Settlements:** One capital city positioned in the northern broader section
**Forest Type:** Appears to be extremely dense old-growth forest with minimal human intervention

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- The southern peninsula is almost entirely uninhabited forest
- A small cleared area around the capital (only 2-3 kilometers)
- The coastline is highly irregular with numerous small bays
- Several tiny offshore islets on the western side
- What appears to be a river or large stream flows from north to south
- Limited evidence of roads or infrastructure beyond the capital
- A few small fishing hamlets on the eastern coast (3-4 structures each)
