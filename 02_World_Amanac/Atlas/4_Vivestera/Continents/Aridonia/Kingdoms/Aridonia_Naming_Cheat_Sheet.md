# Aridonia Naming Cheat Sheet & Cultural Lexicon
**Reference: `Reference_Material/Aridonia_Cultures_Lexicons.md`**

This cheat sheet summarizes the naming conventions for Aridonia's major cultures. Use these roots to generate authentic names for NPCs, locations, and items.

---

## 1. Vorthic (Draconic/Desert) - *The Vorth Empire*
*Values: Honor, Lineage, Survival.*
*   **Roots:**
    *   **Vorth** (Dragon/Ruler)
    *   **Sert** (Sand/Desert)
    *   **Kriv** (Fire/Sun)
    *   **Zal** (Water)
    *   **Aru** (Home/Oasis)
*   **Examples:** *Zal-Aru* (Water-Home), *SasSert* (Great Sand), *Vorth-Loge* (Dragon Fortress).

## 2. Khazdul (Dwarven) - *Ayarson Clans*
*Values: Craft, Stone, Grudges.*
*   **Roots:**
    *   **Khaz** (Mountain/Stone)
    *   **Dum** (Hall/Home)
    *   **Ayar** (Iron/Strong)
    *   **Tor** (Peak)
    *   **Baraz** (Red/Fire)
*   **Examples:** *Ayar-Dum* (Iron-Hall), *Grom-Karak* (Deep-Fortress), *Mithril-Delve*.

## 3. Sylvanic (Green Tongue) - *Sprout Kingdom*
*Values: Nature, Cycles, Secrecy.*
*   **Roots:**
    *   **Sylv** (Forest/Green)
    *   **Bloom** (Flower/Growth)
    *   **Root** (Foundation)
    *   **Whisper** (Secret)
*   **Examples:** *Ever-Bloom* (Capital), *Root-Spire*, *Moss-Path*.

## 4. Rustic (Industrial Common) - *Kingdom of Rust*
*Values: Labor, Metal, Progress.*
*   **Roots:**
    *   **Fer/Iron** (Metal)
    *   **Slag** (Waste/Poor)
    *   **Gear** (Machine)
    *   **Oxide** (Rust/Age)
*   **Examples:** *Ferropolis* (Iron City), *Slag-Town* (Slums), *Oxide-Bay*.

## 5. Maritime Common - *Island Kingdoms (Cerulea, Westallis)*
*Values: Trade, Navigation, Wealth.*
*   **Roots:**
    *   **Marr** (Sea)
    *   **Haven** (Safety)
    *   **Port** (Trade)
    *   **Cerul** (Blue/Noble)
*   **Examples:** *FylloDraya* (River-Crossing), *Westhaven*, *Azure-Hold*.

## 6. Debrican (Pastoral) - *Kingdom of Debrica*
*Values: Land, Seasons, Community.*
*   **Roots:**
    *   **Stag** (Lord)
    *   **Doe** (Mother/Nurture)
    *   **Vale** (Valley)
    *   **Field** (Home)
*   **Examples:** *Stag-Ford*, *Doe-Vale*, *Harvest-Home*.

---
**Tip:** Combine roots to create new names (e.g., *Sert-Kriv* = "Sand-Fire" -> A hot desert region).
