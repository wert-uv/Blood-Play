# EASTERN COASTAL REGION - KINGDOM OF WESTALLIS (Continuation)

**Position:** East-central coast of the continent
**Terrain Type:** Coastal plains with forest and agricultural zones
**Description:** The eastern continuation of Westallis along the coast, where the kingdom meets the ocean. This region shows more intensive human settlement and agricultural development compared to the northern section. The terrain is flatter, with broad river deltas and coastal plains ideal for farming.

**Notable Unnamed Features:**
- Several major ports and harbor towns (5-6 significant settlements)
- Extensive cleared agricultural land in river valleys
- A defensive wall or fortification line may exist along the southern border
- Numerous fishing villages dot the coastline
- River deltas create fertile agricultural zones
- Road network is very developed, connecting all major settlements
- Some wetlands exist in low-lying coastal areas
