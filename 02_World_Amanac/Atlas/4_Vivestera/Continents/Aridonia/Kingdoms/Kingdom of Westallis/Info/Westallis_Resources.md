# 🌲 Kingdom of Westallis - Natural Resources

## ⛏️ Mineral Wealth

### **Coal**
- **Northern Coal Fields:** Bituminous coal deposits.
- **Usage:** Domestic heating and export to coastal cities.

### **Porcelain Clay (Kaolin)**
- **Location:** Weathered granite regions in the north.
- **Product:** Fine white porcelain (Luxury export).

### **Silver**
- **Silver-Falls:** Unique waterfall exposing silver veins.

## 🌲 Forest & Agricultural Wealth

### **Timber**
- **Storm Pine:** Excellent for ship masts and heavy construction.
- **Exports:** Major lumber supplier for the Western Archipelago shipyards.

### **Gems**
- **Emeralds:** Secondary deposits in northern peaks (less common than Sprout Kingdom).

---
**Economic Focus:** Timber, Coal, Fine Porcelain, Silver.
