# 🗺️ Kingdom of Westallis - Geography & Biomes

## 📍 Geographic Profile
- **Region:** Northeastern Eastern Continent.
- **Position:** North of the Central Mountains and Vorth Loge.
- **Terrain:** Highland plateaus, glaciated valleys, and fjords.

## 🌿 Biome: Boreal Forest (Taiga)
- **Climate:** Cold Temperate to Subarctic. Long, snowy winters.
- **Vegetation:** Massive coniferous forests (Spruce, Fir, Pine).
- **Transition:** Southern territories transition to mixed hardwood forests.

## 🏞️ Key Features
- **Northern Coast:** Rocky, fjord-like inlets and deep harbors using natural glacial features.
- **Inland Lakes:** Numerous glacial tarns and cold-water lakes.
- **Eastern Frontier:** Wild, dense old-growth forest extending to the eastern ocean.
- **Rivers:** Fast-flowing, cold rivers draining north/east.
