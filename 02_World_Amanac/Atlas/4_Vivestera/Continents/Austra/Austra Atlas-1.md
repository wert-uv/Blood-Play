## SOUTHWESTERN CONTINENT - AUSTRA

**Position:** Southwestern portion of the visible map
**Geographic Type:** Large continental landmass
**Approximate Size:** 1,200 km north-south by 900 km east-west

### Overall Description
A substantial continent in the southwest, appearing somewhat isolated from other major landmasses. The name "Austra" may suggest "southern" origin, though it's not the southernmost landmass shown.
